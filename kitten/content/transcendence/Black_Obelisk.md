A [Black Obelisk]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Obelisk.md" >}}) is a permanent endgame building found in the [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. Its primary function is to increase the **Solar Revolution production bonus cap**, allowing your kitten civilisation to benefit from higher levels of faith-based productivity.

### **Core Statistics and Costs**
*   **Cost:** 100 Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have attained at least **Transcendence Tier 1**.
*   **Fidelity:** Like all Cryptotheology buildings, Black Obelisks **carry over through resets**; however, you must research the "Cryptotheology" technology in each new run to view or purchase them.

### **The Solar Revolution Cap**
The standard Solar Revolution bonus is typically capped at **1000%**, with diminishing returns beginning at 750%. Black Obelisks are the primary method for raising this ceiling:
*   **Scaling Formula:** Every obelisk improves the cap by **+5% per Transcendence Tier**.
*   **Multiplicative Synergies:** This effect is multiplicative with the rewards gained from completing the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}).

### **Strategic Context**
In the late game (Phase 3), the Black Obelisk is often compared to the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}) reward. Completing the Atheism Challenge provides a base reward that increases the Solar Revolution cap by **100 percentage points per Transcendence Tier**, which the sources note is the equivalent of owning **20 Black Obelisks**. 

For players focusing on a "Relic Engine" run, stacking these obelisks alongside high Transcendence Tiers is essential for maximizing global production modifiers.
