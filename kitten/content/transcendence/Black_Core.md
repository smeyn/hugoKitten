A [Black Core]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Core.md" >}}) is a late-game [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) building that **increases your maximum limit of Black Liquid Sorrow (BLS)** by +1%. This expansion of the sorrow cap is essential for scaling your religious infrastructure as you approach the endgame.

### **Costs and Requirements**
*   **Initial Cost:** 10,000 Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must reach **Transcendence Tier 5** to unlock it.

### **Strategic Utility**
The Black Core is a fundamental piece of the "Relic Engine" because it allows for the continued construction of [Black Pyramids]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Black_Pyramid.md" >}}):
*   **Bypassing the Sorrow Cap:** The base storage cap for Black Liquid Sorrow (BLS) is 16. Since each [Black Pyramid]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Black_Pyramid.md" >}}) requires 5 units of sorrow to build, you are naturally restricted in how many pyramids you can own. 
*   **Scaling Pyramids:** By building Black Cores, you increase the BLS limit, allowing you to accumulate the sorrow necessary to purchase more pyramids.
*   **Synergy with Relic Production:** Increasing your pyramid count is critical for the [Black Nexus]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Nexus.md" >}}). The Black Nexus improves relic production from space beacons and Time Crystal refinement by a factor determined by your total number of Black Pyramids.

### **Operational Context**
The Black Core belongs to **Phase 3 (Late/Endgame)** of development. It is a permanent investment that **carries over through resets**, provided you research the "Cryptotheology" technology in each new run to view the buildings you have purchased. 

At this stage, players typically focus on "chasing infinity" by using these cores to maximise the efficiency of their **Time Crystal and Relic** engines.
