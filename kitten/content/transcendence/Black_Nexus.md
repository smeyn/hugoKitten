A [Black Nexus]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Nexus.md" >}}) is a high-tier [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) building found in the Religion tab that serves as a cornerstone for scaling your late-game relic production. It is designed to make the conversion of Time Crystals into Relics more efficient and to boost the passive generation of relics from space-based structures.

### **Core Mechanics and Costs**
*   **Cost:** 5,000 Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have attained **Transcendence Tier 3** to unlock it.
*   **Synergy:** Its effectiveness is directly tied to the number of [Black Pyramids]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Black_Pyramid.md" >}}) you have constructed.

### **Primary Effects**
The Black Nexus enhances two specific methods of obtaining relics:

1.  **Time Crystal Refinement:** 
    Standard refinement converts 25 Time Crystals into 1 Relic. With a Black Nexus, the yield increases according to the formula: **1 + (Black Nexus × Black Pyramid)** relics per 25 crystals.
2.  **Relic Station Production:** 
    It significantly boosts the output of [Relic Stations]( {{< relref "upgrade/Relic_Station.md" >}}) (upgraded Space Beacons). Each Black Nexus improves this production by **10% per Black Pyramid**.

### **Strategic Utility: The "Type 1 Relic Engine"**
In the mid-to-late game, the Black Nexus is a vital part of what players call the "Type 1 Relic Engine". 

*   **Scaling Passive Income:** Once you have at least 5,000 maximum [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}), Relic Stations produce 0.01 relics per day. By stacking Black Nexuses and Black Pyramids, you can increase this passive daily income to a point where you no longer rely solely on manual trading with Leviathans for your relic supply.
*   **Synergy with Black Sky:** Completing the **Black Sky challenge** provides an "invisible" Black Pyramid, which permanently improves the scaling of your Black Nexus buildings in all future runs.
*   **Late-Game Goal:** Experienced players recommend buying several levels of Black Nexus alongside [Black Core]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Core.md" >}}) (to increase the sorrow limit for more pyramids) to maximise the efficiency of your time-skipping and relic-farming operations.
