[Black Radiance]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Radiance.md" >}}) is an advanced [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) building that accelerates the rate at which [Alicorns]( {{< relref "resource/alicorn.md" >}}) are corrupted into **Necrocorns**. It is a permanent structure that carries over through resets, provided you research the Cryptotheology technology in each new run.

### **Cost and Requirements**
*   **Cost:** 37.5K Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** **Transcendence Tier 12**.

### **Core Effects**
The primary function of Black Radiance is to speed up the **corruption countdown** initiated by Markers. 
*   **Corruption Bonus:** The approximate bonus to corruption speed is **3.464% multiplied by the square root** of (number of Black Radiances $\times$ points of Black Liquid Sorrow).
*   **Synergy:** Because the bonus depends on your current **Black Liquid Sorrow (BLS)**, its effectiveness increases as you accumulate more sorrow or build [Black Cores]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Core.md" >}}) to raise your sorrow limit.

### **Strategic Utility**
In the late-game "Relic Engine" phase, Black Radiance is used to optimize the generation of **Necrocorns**:
*   **Feeding Leviathans:** Faster corruption provides a steadier supply of Necrocorns to feed to the [Leviathans]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Leviathans.md" >}}), which permanently increases their **energy cap**.
*   **Trade Efficiency:** Higher Leviathan energy results in more [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) and [Relics]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}) per trade, making Black Radiance a vital component for accelerating the growth of your endgame resources.
