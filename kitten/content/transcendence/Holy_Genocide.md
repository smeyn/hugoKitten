[Holy Genocide]( {{< relref "transcendence/Holy_Genocide.md" >}}) is the final building in the [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. It represents the absolute pinnacle of the endgame, requiring an immense investment of resources and spiritual progression to unlock.

### **Requirements and Costs**
Building a Holy Genocide is significantly more expensive than any other religious structure and is gated by the highest Transcendence Tier in the game.

*   **Relic Cost:** 100,000 Relics.
*   **Void Cost:** 25,000 Void.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** **Transcendence Tier 25** (requiring approximately 66.96G% Epiphany).

### **Primary Effects**
The building alters the fundamental math of your kitten population and production to favor high-efficiency resets.

*   **Population Penalty:** It reduces your maximum kitten capacity by **1% per level** (with diminishing returns starting at 75%).
*   **Prestige Boost:** It provides a **+2% bonus to Karma and Paragon** gained per kitten upon resetting.
*   **Production Compensation:** To offset the lower population, it grants **+2% kitten production per housing building**. 
*   **Zebra Synergy:** For players in Iron Will mode, the building provides the **2% production boost to Zebras** without the accompanying decrease in maximum population.

### **Unlocking the Post Apocalypse Challenge**
The most significant utility of Holy Genocide is that it serves as the unlock requirement for the **Post Apocalypse** challenge.

*   **The Challenge:** You enter a timeline with **10M ppm of pollution** where kittens arrive 10 times slower and natural pollution dissipation is disabled.
*   **The Goal:** You must clear all pollution within the broken timeline.
*   **The Rewards:** Successful completion increases the number of **cryochambers** that can be operational in future runs and unlocks special policies. Each subsequent completion adds +1 to the cryochamber support cap.

### **Strategic Context**
Holy Genocide is considered a "Phase 3" or **Endgame** structure. Because the building and your Transcendence Tier carry over through resets, it is used by advanced players to "chase infinity" by overcapping resources and maximizing the efficiency of **Paragon farming**. By the time you can afford this building, you are likely using a **Type 2 Relic Engine** or a **Shatter Engine** to generate the necessary thousands of relics and void units required for construction.
