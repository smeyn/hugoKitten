The [Black Library]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Library.md" >}}) is an advanced [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) building used to scale your **science storage cap** by enhancing the effectiveness of your existing religious and scientific upgrades. It is a permanent structure that carries over through resets, allowing for exponential growth in science capacity across multiple runs.

### **Requirements and Costs**
Because it is part of the endgame "Phase 3" progression, the requirements for the Black Library are steep:
*   **Cost:** 30,000 Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have attained **Transcendence Tier 9**.
*   **Technology:** You must research the [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) technology each run to view or purchase these buildings.

---

### **Primary Effect: Codex Scaling**
The Black Library focuses on improving the **Codex Leviathanianus**, a metaphysics upgrade that increases your compendia science limit based on your Transcendence tier.
*   **Scaling Bonus:** Each Black Library improves the *Codex Leviathanianus* scaling by **+2%**.
*   **Science Storage:** This allows [Compendiums]( {{< relref "resource/Compendium.md" >}}) to continue providing +10 science storage even after you have reached the standard science cap provided by buildings.

---

### **Synergies and Advanced Scaling**
The Black Library is highly integrated with other late-game religious and industrial systems:

| Synergy Component | Effect on Black Library |
| :--- | :--- |
| [Unicorn Graveyard]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Graveyard.md" >}}) | Directly improves the bonus provided by the Black Library. |
| **Pact of Purity** | Increases the Black Library ratio by **+0.05% for every Black Pyramid** you own. |
| [Transcendence]( {{< relref "religion/Transcendence.md" >}}) | As your Transcendence tier increases, the base effectiveness of the Codex it scales also rises. |

### **Strategic Context**
In the larger context of a "Relic Engine" run, the Black Library is essential for bypassing the science caps required for the most expensive endgame technologies, such as [Exophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Exophysics.md" >}}) (25M Science) or advanced space structures. By stacking Black Libraries and [Unicorn Graveyards]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Graveyard.md" >}}), you ensure that your stockpile of Compendiums provides a massive, stable foundation for your scientific research.
