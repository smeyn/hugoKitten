[Dark Nova]( {{< relref "transcendence/Dark_Nova.md" >}}) is a permanent endgame **Cryptotheology building** that increases your civilisation's **global energy production** by **+2%** per level. Like all Cryptotheology structures, it carries over through resets, although you must re-research the "Cryptotheology" technology in each new run to view or purchase additional units.

### **Costs and Requirements**
Building a Dark Nova represents a significant investment in late-game resources and spiritual progression.

*   **Relic Cost:** 75,000 Relics.
*   **Void Cost:** 7,500 Void.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have attained **Transcendence Tier 20** to unlock this building. Reaching Tier 20 requires an accumulation of approximately **451.14M% Epiphany**.

---

### **Strategic Utility**
Dark Nova is situated in the final phase of the game (Phase 3/Endgame), where energy management becomes a primary bottleneck for scientific and industrial expansion.

*   **Endgame Power Demands:** As you progress toward the "positive reset" loop, you must power high-consumption buildings like [AI Cores]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}) (which consume increasing energy per unit), **Containment Chambers** (50 Watts each), and **Entanglement Stations** (25 Watts each). The +2% global production bonus helps sustain these power-hungry systems.
*   **Scaling for Infinity:** Advanced players utilize the energy boost to support massive arrays of [Sunlifters]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) and [Hydro Plants]( {{< relref "buildings/Hydro_Plant.md" >}}) while attempting to reach the **8.14 billion Unobtainium capacity** required for 100 Chronospheres.
*   **Synergy with Void Rifts:** The global energy production bonus stacks with other modifiers, helping to maintain a positive energy surplus during **Time Skips**, which is necessary for [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) generation and the automated "shatter engine".

### **Building Tier Context**
In the progression of Cryptotheology, Dark Nova is one of the final buildings unlocked, coming after other critical structures like [Blazars]( {{< relref "transcendence/Blazar.md" >}}) (Tier 15) but before [Mausoleums]( {{< relref "transcendence/Mausoleum.md" >}}) (Tier 23) and [Holy Genocide]( {{< relref "transcendence/Holy_Genocide.md" >}}) (Tier 25). It is a foundational component for players seeking to "chase infinity" by overcapping resources through thousands of years of skipped time.
