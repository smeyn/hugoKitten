A [Blazar]( {{< relref "transcendence/Blazar.md" >}}) is a high-tier **Cryptotheology building** that serves as a cornerstone for the late-game "time crystal engine". It is a permanent structure that carries over through resets, though you must research the Cryptotheology technology in each new run to access its benefits.

### **Requirements and Cost**
Because it is a pinnacle endgame structure, the barriers to entry are significant:
*   **Cost:** 50,000 Relics.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** **Transcendence Tier 15**. Reaching this tier requires a massive accumulation of Epiphany (roughly 3.04 million percent).

### **Core Effects and Multipliers**
Each level of Blazar provides stacking bonuses to several critical late-game mechanics:
*   **Resource Retrieval:** Improves effectiveness by **2%**. This is vital for "shatter engines," as it increases the resources gained when you skip years using Time Crystals.
*   **Time Manipulation:** Improves [Chronomancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Chronomancy.md" >}}) by **2.5%**.
*   **Unicorn Generation:** Improves [Unicornmancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Unicornmancy.md" >}}) by **2.5%**.
*   **Dark Future Mitigation:** Improves **Time Impedance** by **10%**. This helps suppress the temporal penalties encountered once your calendar year exceeds 40,000.

### **Strategic Utility**
The Blazar is primarily used to transition into the **"Type 2 Relic Engine"** and beyond. 

*   **Shatter Engine Efficiency:** By boosting Resource Retrieval, Blazars allow you to reach a "break-even" point where shattering a Time Crystal provides enough Unobtainium or Alicorns to immediately regain that crystal through trading or sacrifice.
*   **Relic Scaling:** In the late game, relics are often generated by refining Time Crystals. Blazars, along with Black Nexuses, make this process far more productive, eventually allowing for the "positive resets" necessary to reach the theoretical limits of the game.
*   **Infinite Loop Resets:** For players aiming to achieve **"Infinity"**, Blazars are used to grow the Unobtainium stockpile exponentially across short runs, enabling the construction of up to 100 Chronospheres for massive resource carry-over.

While reaching Transcendence Tier 15 is a daunting task, the Blazar is the primary tool used to overcome the late-game bottlenecks of [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) and [Relics]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}).
