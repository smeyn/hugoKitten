[Void]( {{< relref "resource/void.md" >}}) is a specialized, late-game resource in *Kittens Game* primarily used to unlock advanced temporal and scientific mechanics. Unlike common resources, it cannot be produced by kittens or buildings; instead, it is **randomly generated during "temporal paradoxes"** that occur if you have constructed [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}).

### **Acquisition and Generation**
*   **Temporal Paradoxes:** Void is gained only during these events. The generation rate is notoriously slow, making it a major late-game bottleneck.
*   **Scaling Generation:** Having more Chronospheres increases the frequency and amount of void generated. 
*   **Void Hoover:** This structure (costing 250 void and 10 time crystals) increases the maximum amount of void you can gain during a single paradox day.
*   **Chronocontrol:** Researching this increases the duration of temporal paradoxes, providing more opportunities for void to accumulate.
*   **Preservation:** As a non-luxury, non-crafted resource, 1.5% of your current void is preserved per Chronosphere upon resetting the game.

### **Technological and Scientific Progression**
Void is the mandatory gatekeeper for Phase 3 ("Endgame") technologies and several high-tier sciences:
*   **Void Space (800k Science, 30 Time Crystals, 100 Void):** This science is the prerequisite for attempting the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}) and for unlocking the ability to build **Cryochambers**.
*   **Paradox Theory (1M Science, 40 Time Crystals, 250 Void):** Unlocks the most advanced time-manipulation tools, including **Resource Retrieval**, [Distortion]( {{< relref "upgrade/Distortion.md" >}}), and **Void Resonators**.
*   [Void Aspiration:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Aspiration.md" >}}) A workshop upgrade that unlocks **Void Hoovers** and **Void Rifts**.

### **Key Benefits and Multipliers**
*   [Happiness:]( {{< relref "guides/Happiness.md" >}}) Simply holding any amount of void provides a flat **+10% happiness bonus** to your kittens.
*   **Storage Capacity:** **Void Rifts** (initially costing 75 void) increase your **global resource storage ratio by 2%**. This effect stacks additively with Event Horizons. In the extreme endgame, building approximately 50 Void Rifts is a common goal for resource scaling.
*   **Energy Production:** Each Void Rift increases the energy production of **HR Harvesters** by **10%**.
*   **Science Scaling:** Void is required for the [Void Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Reactors.md" >}}) upgrade, which increases the maximum science capacity of Research Vessels and Space Beacons by **400%**.

### **Advanced Infrastructure Costs**
Void is required for constructing and maintaining endgame structures:
*   **Cryochambers:** Building one costs 100 void, and **fixing a used cryochamber** costs 500 void.
*   [Dark Nova:]( {{< relref "transcendence/Dark_Nova.md" >}}) An endgame cryptotheology building costing 7,500 void and 75k relics; it provides a 2% boost to global energy production.
*   [Holy Genocide:]( {{< relref "transcendence/Holy_Genocide.md" >}}) A high-tier upgrade costing 25,000 void and 100k relics.
*   [Chronosurge:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronosurge.md" >}}) Costs 750 void and enables Chronospheres to generate temporal flux yearly.
*   **Temporal Press:** Used to fine-tune time-crystal shattering, these cost 10 void each.
