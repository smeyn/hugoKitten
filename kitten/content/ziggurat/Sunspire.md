A [Sunspire]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Sunspire.md" >}}) is a high-tier endgame religious building found in the Ziggurat section of the Religion tab. It represents one of the final stages in the unicorn-to-time-crystal pipeline, offering massive bonuses to unicorn generation and alicorn summoning.

### **Statistics and Requirements**
To construct a Sunspire, you must first have at least one [Unicorn Utopia]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Utopia.md" >}}). 

*   **Cost:** 25,000 Unicorn Tears, 750,000 Ivory, and 1,000 Gold.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Production Bonus:** It provides a massive **+500% bonus to unicorn generation**.

### **Core Effects**
The Sunspire is critical for the late-game "Unicorn Era", as it automates and enhances the production of rare celestial resources:

*   **Alicorn Generation:** It provides a passive **0.00005 alicorns per tick** (0.2 per year) and increases the random alicorn summon chance by **0.03% per game-day**.
*   **Time Crystal Efficiency:** It improves the **Time Crystal (TC) refine ratio by 10%**, meaning you receive more Time Crystals when sacrificing alicorns.
*   **Ivory Harvesting:** It increases the ivory gained from **Ivory Meteors** (a random event summoned by Ivory Citadels) by **50%**.

### **Strategic Utility**
In the progression toward a self-sustaining "Positive Reset" engine, Sunspires are used to overcome the massive resource requirements of the late game.

*   **Build Order:** Strategic guides recommend first building a solid foundation of roughly **25 Ivory Citadels** before prioritising higher-tier structures like Unicorn Utopias and Sunspires.
*   **Synergy:** The effectiveness of a Sunspire is heavily reliant on your [Solar Revolution]( {{< relref "religion/Solar_Revolution.md" >}}) bonus. By the time you are constructing these, you should aim to have your faith production near the **1000% cap** to maximise unicorn growth.
*   **Relic Engine:** Sunspires are a foundational component of the "**Type 0 Relic Engine**", where increased unicorn and alicorn production allows for more frequent sacrifices to generate the Time Crystals and Relics needed for [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}).
