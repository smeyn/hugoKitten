The [Unicorn Graveyard]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Graveyard.md" >}}) is an endgame building found within the [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. It is primarily used to scale your maximum culture capacity based on your religious infrastructure.

### **Core Statistics and Costs**
*   **Cost:** 5 Necrocorns and 1000 Megaliths.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisites:** You must have unlocked the **Black Codex** (a metaphysics upgrade costing 25 Paragon that requires Megalomania).

### **Primary Effects**
*   **Culture Scaling:** Increases your **maximum Culture by +1% for every Ziggurat** you own.
*   **Cryptotheology Synergy:** It provides a bonus to the [Black Library]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Library.md" >}}) (another cryptotheology building), specifically improving the scaling of the *Codex Leviathanianus*. 
*   **Flavor Text:** It is poetically described in-game as the "Grave of the fireflies".

### **Strategic Value**
In the late game, [Culture]( {{< relref "resource/culture.md" >}}) often becomes a significant bottleneck for technological advancement and crafting [Manuscripts]( {{< relref "resource/manuscript.md" >}}). Because your Ziggurat count can become quite high during the "Unicorn Era" as you hunt for Time Crystals, the Unicorn Graveyard provides a powerful way to expand your culture cap without relying solely on standard buildings like Amphitheatres or Temples. 

The requirement of **Necrocorns** means you must already have established [Markers]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Marker.md" >}}) to corrupt your Alicorns, placing this building firmly in the late-game "Phase 3" of development.
