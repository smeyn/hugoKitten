An [Ivory Citadel]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Citadel.md" >}}) is a mid-to-high-tier religious building found in the [Ziggurat]( {{< relref "buildings/Ziggurat.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. It is a critical component of the "Unicorn Era", serving as an upgrade to the Ivory Tower and a stepping stone toward advanced structures like Sky Palaces and Sunspires.

### **Cost and Requirements**
*   **Initial Cost:** 50 Unicorn Tears and 50,000 Ivory.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15 (meaning each subsequent citadel costs 15% more).
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have constructed at least one [Ivory Tower]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Tower.md" >}}).

### **Core Effects**
The Ivory Citadel provides two primary benefits to your civilisation:
*   **Unicorn Production:** It increases your base unicorn generation by **+25% per tick**.
*   **Ivory Meteors:** It unlocks a random event called an **Ivory Meteor**. Each citadel provides a **0.05% chance per game-day** to summon a meteor that grants a lump sum of ivory upon impact.

### **Strategic Analysis**
Managing your Ivory Citadels is essential for transitioning from basic resource gathering to the advanced time-manipulation phase of the game.

*   **The "25 Citadel" Rule:** Experienced players recommend **optimising for ivory** early in the religious grind. A common strategy is to build approximately **25 Ivory Citadels** before focusing heavily on Unicorn Utopias or Sunspires. This provides a stable enough ivory income (via meteors) to fund more expensive endgame structures.
*   **AFK Resource Generation:** Because Ivory Meteors provide a passive source of ivory, Citadels are highly valued for **"AFK" playstyles** where you may be away from the computer for long periods. 
*   **Synergy with Mints:** While Mints also produce ivory, their output does not scale with paragon or religious buildings. Ivory Citadels, by contrast, accelerate the unicorn-to-tear pipeline, which is necessary to eventually unlock [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) and [Relics]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}) through Leviathan trading.
