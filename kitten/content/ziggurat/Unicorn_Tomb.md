A [Unicorn Tomb]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Tomb.md" >}}) is an early-tier building found in the Ziggurat section of the Religion tab that provides a 5% bonus to **unicorn generation**. It is the first building in a progression chain that leads to more powerful structures like Ivory Towers and Sky Palaces.

### **Core Statistics and Costs**
*   **Cost:** 5 Unicorn Tears and 500 Ivory.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisites:** You must have unlocked [Ivory]( {{< relref "resource/ivory.md" >}}) and **Unicorn Tears** (typically by building a Ziggurat and sacrificing unicorns).
*   **Primary Effect:** Increases unicorns per tick by **+5%**.

### **Challenge Reward**
The Unicorn Tomb gains a significant secondary utility through the **Unicorn Tears challenge**. Upon successfully completing this challenge, Unicorn Tombs provide a permanent base reward: they increase your **maximum faith capacity** based on your current **Transcendence Tier**. This makes them relevant even in the late game when the 5% unicorn production bonus becomes less impactful compared to higher-tier buildings like Sunspires.

### **Strategic Context**
*   **Early Unicorn Era:** In the beginning of the unicorn-to-time-crystal pipeline, Unicorn Tombs are essential for accelerating your production rate so you can reach the 2,500 unicorns required for further sacrifices.
*   **Scaling:** Like other religious buildings, its cost increases with each one built. Strategic guides recommend using a **unicorn calculator** to determine if a Unicorn Tomb or another building, like an Ivory Tower, offers the best return on investment for your current resources.
*   **Religion Integration:** In later runs, once you have higher [Solar Revolution]( {{< relref "religion/Solar_Revolution.md" >}}) bonuses, your unicorn production will scale significantly, and these tombs serve as a foundational layer for that growth.
