The [Unicorn Necropolis]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Necropolis.md" >}}) is an advanced endgame building found in the [Cryptotheology]( {{< relref "techs/Cryptotheology.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. It is primarily used to accelerate the generation of **Necrocorns** by increasing the rate of alicorn corruption.

### **Core Statistics and Costs**

| Attribute | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Cost** | 15 Necrocorns, 2500 Megaliths, 100 Alicorns, and 5 Void |
| **Price Ratio** | 1.15 |
| **Prerequisite** | [Unicorn Graveyard]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Graveyard.md" >}}) |
| **Primary Effect** | Increases **Necrocorn corruption** speed by **10%** |

### **Mechanics and Synergy**
The Unicorn Necropolis functions by speeding up the "corruption" process that transforms your [Alicorns]( {{< relref "resource/alicorn.md" >}}) into **Necrocorns**. 
*   **Corruption Loop:** While [Markers]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Marker.md" >}}) initiate the ticking corruption countdown, the Necropolis serves as a multiplier for that speed.
*   **The "One Necrocorn" Rule:** For these buildings to function, you must maintain a balance of **at least 1 Necrocorn** in your inventory.

### **Strategic Utility and ROI**
Because the building itself costs Necrocorns—which are otherwise used to feed [Leviathans]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Leviathans.md" >}}) to increase their energy cap—there is a specific threshold for when they become a "profitable" investment.

*   **The Break-Even Point:** Strategic guides suggest that the Unicorn Necropolis only becomes truly profitable at **11 units**. 
*   **Opportunity Cost:** For the first 9 or 10 buildings, the rate at which Necrocorns are generated is slower than the rate at which you would have gained benefits by simply feeding those Necrocorns directly to the Leviathans. 
*   **Total Investment:** Reaching this profitability threshold (11 Necropolises) requires a total investment of **365 Necrocorns**.

### **Endgame Context**
The building is described as "The ocean of blood at the end of the lane". It represents the final stage of the unicorn-to-necrocorn pipeline, allowing players to eventually automate the feeding of [Leviathans]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Leviathans.md" >}}) at high speeds to maximize trade ratios for [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) and [Relics]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}).
