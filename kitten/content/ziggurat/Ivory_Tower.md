An [Ivory Tower]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Tower.md" >}}) is a religious structure found in the [Ziggurat]( {{< relref "buildings/Ziggurat.md" >}}) section of the Religion tab. It serves as a mid-tier building in the unicorn-to-time-crystal pipeline, primarily used to accelerate unicorn production and unlock rare celestial events.

### **Core Statistics and Costs**
*   **Cost:** 25 Unicorn Tears and 25,000 Ivory.
*   **Price Ratio:** 1.15.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have constructed at least one [Unicorn Tomb]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Unicorn_Tomb.md" >}}).

### **Primary Effects**
*   **Unicorn Generation:** Each tower provides a **+10% bonus** to your unicorns produced per tick.
*   **Unicorn Rifts:** This is the most significant feature of the building. It unlocks a random event called a **Unicorn Rift**, which provides a large lump sum of unicorns instantly.

### **Unicorn Rift Mechanics**
The Ivory Tower is the only way to trigger rifts, which become more frequent and powerful as your civilisation grows:
*   **Frequency:** Each tower adds a **0.05% chance per game-day** for a rift to occur. For example, building 20 towers ensures a 1% chance every day.
*   **Yield Scaling:** While the base rift reward is 500 unicorns, it is boosted by **10% of your total unicorn ziggurat production bonus**. 

### **Strategic Progression**
In the broader "Unicorn Era" strategy, the Ivory Tower is the second step in a long chain of increasingly expensive buildings:
1.  Unicorn Tomb (+5% unicorns).
2.  [Ivory Tower]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Tower.md" >}}) (+10% unicorns + Rifts).
3.  [Ivory Citadel]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Citadel.md" >}}) (+25% unicorns + Ivory Meteors).
4.  [Sky Palace]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Sky_Palace.md" >}}) (+50% unicorns + Alicorn generation).

To maximize your returns, it is often recommended to use a **unicorn calculator** to decide when to stop building towers and start saving for [Ivory Citadels]( {{< relref "ziggurat/Ivory_Citadel.md" >}}), which provide a much larger production boost and allow you to farm ivory through meteors.
