In *Kittens Game*, the **Engineer** is a specialised kitten job focused on **automating the crafting process** in the Workshop. 

### **Unlocking and Assignment**
*   **Prerequisite:** To assign kittens as Engineers, you must first research the **Mechanization** technology (costs 115k Science and 45 Blueprints).
*   **Assignment:** Once unlocked, kittens can be assigned to the Engineer job in the Village tab.
*   **Note on Traits:** The **Artisan** trait was previously named "Engineer" but was changed to avoid confusion with this job role. While the Artisan trait provides a global bonus to crafting efficiency, the Engineer job performs the physical automation of crafts.

### **Core Functionality: Automation**
The primary role of Engineers is to **automate the conversion of resources** that would otherwise hit their storage caps.
*   **Crafting Points:** Each assigned kitten provides "Engineer points" to the Workshop's automated crafting pool.
*   **Resource Management:** They are particularly vital for managing resources like **Oil**, which they can automatically convert into **Kerosene** to prevent production from halting due to full storage.
*   **Late-Game Utility:** As the game progresses and manual crafting becomes tedious, players typically shift a larger portion of their population into Engineer and Priest roles to handle massive resource throughput.

### **Upgrades and Effectiveness**
The efficiency of Engineers—often measured in "know-how"—can be significantly enhanced through various Workshop upgrades:
*   **Know-How Progression:** Multiple upgrades increase the effectiveness of different "classes" of Engineers:
    *   **Factory Optimization:** Increases know-how for Class 1 (10) and Class 2 (2).
    *   **Space Engineers:** Increases know-how for Class 1 (10), Class 2 (5), and adds Class 3 and 4 (2 each).
    *   **AI Engineers:** Further expands know-how across classes 1 through 5.
    *   **Factory Robotics:** Provides another boost to Classes 1, 2, and 3.
*   **Neural Network:** This critical upgrade **doubles Engineer effectiveness** and allows kittens to learn skills from the "noosphere".
*   **Antimatter Fission:** Specifically makes Engineers **25% more effective at producing Eludium**.

### **Temporal Capabilities**
In the late game, Engineers gain the ability to function outside of normal time:
*   **Chronoengineers:** This upgrade allows Engineers to **continue crafting during time skips** (combusting Time Crystals). 
*   **Resource Retrieval Synergy:** When working during a time skip, their crafting is based on the **Resource Retrieval** level, ensuring they can produce materials even as years are bypassed.
