[Chronomancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Chronomancy.md" >}}) is a mid-game **Metaphysics upgrade** that serves as a vital bridge toward advanced temporal and astronomical technologies. It costs **25 Paragon** to purchase and is primarily used to increase the frequency of celestial phenomena.

### **Core Effects and Unlocks**
*   **Event Multiplier:** It provides a **1.1x multiplier** to the daily chance of both meteors and astronomical events. The sources explicitly note that this is a multiplicative bonus to the base chance, not a flat +10% addition.
*   **Technological Prerequisites:** Purchasing Chronomancy is required to unlock two essential high-tier Metaphysics:
    *   [Anachronomancy:]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Anachronomancy.md" >}}) Allows Time Crystals and Chronophysics to persist across game resets.
    *   **Astromancy:** Further doubles the chance of star events and increases the effectiveness of Observatories.

### **Strategic Utility**
The value of Chronomancy depends on your current stage of progression:
*   **The Starchart Solution:** In early runs where **Starchart production** is a bottleneck, the combination of Chronomancy and Astromancy is described as "pure magic". It more than doubles event frequency, allowing you to build a larger fleet of [Trade Ships]( {{< relref "craft/Trade_Ship.md" >}}) for Titanium acquisition.
*   **Late-Game Priority:** In very advanced "Endgame" or "Iron Will" runs, some strategies suggest prioritising price-ratio reductions (like Renaissance) first, treating Chronomancy as a "less vital" upgrade to be picked up once your economic foundation is stable.
