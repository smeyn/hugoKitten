The [Basilica]( {{< relref "religion/Basilica.md" >}}) is a late-game upgrade available for the [Temple]( {{< relref "buildings/Temple.md" >}}) building, focusing on significantly increasing your cultural resources.

Here are the details regarding the Basilica upgrade:

### Unlocking and Cost

| Detail | Specification |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Building Type** | Upgrade for [Temple]( {{< relref "buildings/Temple.md" >}}). |
| **Prerequisite** | Requires **10,000 Worship**. |
| **Cost** | **1250 Faith** and **750 Gold**. |
| **Price Ratio** | 2.5. |

### Effects on Temples

The Basilica primarily boosts the cultural output and storage capacity provided by your Temples:

*   **Culture Production:** Increases Temple **Culture per Tick** by **$+0.2$**.
*   **Culture Storage:** Increases Temple **Max Culture** by **$+125$**.

### Transcendence Scaling

The Basilica is one of the religious abilities that can be purchased multiple times using [Transcendence]( {{< relref "religion/Transcendence.md" >}}) tiers, which are acquired by spending [Epiphany]( {{< relref "religion/epiphany.md" >}}).

*   For each additional level purchased via Transcendence, the Temples gain an extra **$+0.05$ Culture per Tick** and **$+50$ Max Culture**.
