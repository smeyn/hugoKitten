**Dark Brew** is a specific **Zebra Upgrade** available in the Workshop tab. 

### **Acquisition and Costs**
To purchase this upgrade, you must meet the following requirements:
*   **Prerequisite Building:** You must have constructed at least one **Brewery**.
*   **Resource Costs:**
    *   **Bloodstone:** 1
    *   **Parchment:** 3,000
    *   **Science:** 100

### **Effects**
The primary function of Dark Brew is to link **Festivals** with your zebra population growth. When this upgrade is active, holding a festival provides a **boost to the chance of zebra arrival**. Specifically, the "festival arrival rate" (the speed at which new kittens normally arrive during a festival) also functions as a **multiplier** for the zebra arrival chance.

### **Strategic Context**
This upgrade is part of the late-game zebra progression system, which is particularly relevant in **Iron Will** mode where zebras serve as your primary labor force. Because Bloodstones are rare resources (obtained from hunting with at least 10 zebras or crafted from Time Crystals and Relics), Dark Brew represents an advanced stage of zebra accumulation. By using the **Brewery** to run festivals, players can significantly accelerate the rate at which they reach higher zebra counts, which in turn provides more **Catpower** and better hunting yields.
