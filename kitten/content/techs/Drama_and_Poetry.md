[Drama and Poetry]( {{< relref "techs/Drama_and_Poetry.md" >}}) is a mid-game technology that moves your civilisation from basic resource management into cultural and temporal optimization. It is primarily known for unlocking the **Festival** mechanic and the [Brewery]( {{< relref "buildings/Brewery.md" >}}) building.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
Because of its high requirement for a specific crafted resource, it is often seen as a significant hurdle for new players.
*   **Science Cost:** 90,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 5,000 [Parchment]( {{< relref "craft/Parchment.md" >}}).
*   **Prerequisite:** Researching [Acoustics]( {{< relref "techs/Acoustics.md" >}}).

### **Unlocked Mechanics**
*   [Festivals:]( {{< relref "guides/festivals.md" >}}) Once researched, you can spend resources to hold a festival that lasts for one game year (400 days, or 800 real-time seconds). 
    *   **Kitten Growth:** Festivals are the primary way to **double the arrival speed of kittens** (from ~0.16 to 0.32 kittens per second), which is essential for reaching the population thresholds needed for Paragon.
    *   **Numeromancy Synergies:** If the [Numeromancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/numeromancy.md" >}}) Metaphysics upgrade is researched, holding a festival grants massive global production multipliers based on the current **Cycle** (e.g., x2 Unobtainium in Redmoon, x2 Science in Piscine, or x2 Catpower in Cath).
*   [Brewery:]( {{< relref "buildings/Brewery.md" >}}) A building that enhances the effectiveness of your festivals.
    *   **Cost:** 1,000 Wood, 750 Culture, 5 Spice, and 375 Parchment.
    *   **Effects:** Provides a **+1% bonus** to Festival Happiness and the Festival Cycle Bonus, while increasing Festival Kitten Speed by **+0.1%**.
    *   **Upkeep:** Each active Brewery consumes **-1 Catnip** and **-0.1 Spice** per tick.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Early Game Skip:** Experienced players recommend **skipping this tech** on your first few runs because the 5,000 Parchment cost is considered "waaaay too expensive" to be useful when your economy is still weak.
*   **Mid-to-Late Game Priority:** It becomes vital when you start pursuing **Unobtainium farming** or **rapid Paragon grinding**. In short runs (approx. 30 minutes), players often stack multiple festivals to ensure kittens fill housing as fast as it is built.
*   **Parchment Grinding:** To prepare for this tech, it is recommended to stockpile at least **7,500 Parchment** so you can afford the research and immediately hold several festivals to see the benefit.
