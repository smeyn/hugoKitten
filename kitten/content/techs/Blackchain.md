[Blackchain]( {{< relref "techs/Blackchain.md" >}}) is an extreme late-game science technology that serves as the gateway to the speculative cryptocurrency market within *Kittens Game*.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock Blackchain, you must first research [Quantum Cryptography]( {{< relref "techs/Quantum_Cryptography.md" >}}). The technology is exceptionally expensive, requiring:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 5,000,000 (5M)
*   [Relics:]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}) 4,096

### **Core Functionality**
The primary effect of researching Blackchain is that it **unlocks Blackcoin**, a unique and uncommon resource. Blackcoin functions as a speculative cryptocurrency that is bought and sold from the [Leviathans]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Leviathans.md" >}}) in exchange for relics. Its price fluctuates over time, and these price stats are preserved even when you reset the game.

### **Associated Upgrades**
Researching Blackchain is also a mandatory prerequisite for the [Invisible Black Hand]( {{< relref "upgrade/Invisible_Black_Hand.md" >}}) Workshop upgrade.
*   **Cost:** 128 Time Crystals, 64 Blackcoin, 32 Void, and 4,096 Temporal Flux.
*   **Effect:** This upgrade grants you the ability to **force a blackcoin market crash** at the cost of time crystals, providing a level of control over the speculative market.

Because of the massive relic requirement, Blackchain is typically one of the final scientific milestones achieved in the "Relic Era" or during high-paragon endgame runs.
