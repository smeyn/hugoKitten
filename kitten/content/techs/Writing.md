[Writing]( {{< relref "techs/Writing.md" >}}) is a foundational mid-game science that costs **3,600 Science** and requires [Engineering]( {{< relref "techs/Engineering.md" >}}) as a prerequisite. It is widely identified as the first major **"speedbump"** or bottleneck in the game, marking the transition into a phase where progression relies on complex resource management rather than simple manual gathering.

### **Technological and Structural Unlocks**
Researching Writing provides access to essential tools for cultural and industrial expansion:
*   [Parchment:]( {{< relref "craft/Parchment.md" >}}) Writing enables the crafting of [Parchment]( {{< relref "craft/Parchment.md" >}}) in the Workshop. Parchment is produced using [Furs]( {{< relref "resource/Fur.md" >}}), which are primarily obtained through the **Hunt** system using Catpower.
*   [Amphitheatres:]( {{< relref "buildings/Amphitheatre.md" >}}) This technology allows for the construction of Amphitheatres, which are the earliest buildings used to generate [Culture]( {{< relref "resource/culture.md" >}}). Crucially, they also **mitigate unhappiness** caused by population growth, which is vital for maintaining kitten productivity.
*   **Science Progression:** Writing is the mandatory prerequisite for the [Philosophy]( {{< relref "techs/Philosophy.md" >}}), [Machinery]( {{< relref "techs/Machinery.md" >}}), and [Steel]( {{< relref "craft/Steel.md" >}}) technologies. 
*   [Register:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Register.md" >}}) This Workshop upgrade becomes available, allowing leaders to manage jobs by prioritizing kittens with higher experience.

### **Strategic Utility**
The primary role of Writing is to establish the **"Cultural Engine"** required for the mid-game. Parchment is the mandatory base material for [Manuscripts]( {{< relref "resource/manuscript.md" >}}), which are required in large quantities to unlock critical sciences such as [Theology]( {{< relref "techs/Theology.md" >}}), [Astronomy]( {{< relref "techs/Astronomy.md" >}}), and [Navigation]( {{< relref "techs/Navigation.md" >}}).

Because this phase is gated by furs and culture, common strategies include:
*   **Hunting Optimization:** Temporarily assigning a large portion of the population as [Hunters]( {{< relref "job/Hunter.md" >}}) to generate the Catpower needed for mass Parchment crafting.
*   **Policy Selection:** Enacting the [Tradition]( {{< relref "Policy/Tradition.md" >}}) policy (which costs 150 Culture) to reduce the cost of crafting Manuscripts and double their maximum culture effect, significantly accelerating scientific throughput.
*   **Happiness Management:** Building Amphitheatres until they stop "noticeably improving" happiness to offset the production penalties of a growing village.
