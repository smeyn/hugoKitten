In *Kittens Game*, "currency" can refer to the [Currency]( {{< relref "techs/Currency.md" >}}) technology, the primary economic resource [Gold]( {{< relref "resource/Gold.md" >}}), or the prestige-based currencies used for long-term progression.

### **The Currency Technology**
[Currency]( {{< relref "techs/Currency.md" >}}) is a mid-early game science technology that costs **2,200 Science** and is unlocked after researching [Civil Service]( {{< relref "techs/Civil_Service.md" >}}). Researching it provides the following:
*   **Building Unlock:** Enables the construction of [Tradeposts]( {{< relref "buildings/Tradepost.md" >}}), which reduce luxury resource demand and improve trading effectiveness.
*   **Workshop Upgrade:** Unlocks [Gold Ore]( {{< relref "upgrade/Gold_Ore.md" >}}), which allows your [Smelters]( {{< relref "buildings/Smelter.md" >}}) to produce a small amount of gold (0.001 per tick) while they are active. 

### **Gold (Primary Economic Currency)**
Gold is the standard currency used for domestic improvements and international relations.
*   **Production:** Initially generated by [Smelters]( {{< relref "buildings/Smelter.md" >}}) with the Gold Ore upgrade. Later, **Geologist kittens** can produce it once the [Geodesy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Geodesy.md" >}}) workshop upgrade is purchased. In **Iron Will** mode, hunting also has a 25% chance of yielding gold.
*   **Usage:**
    *   [Trading:]( {{< relref "guides/trading.md" >}}) Used as a cost for discovering new civilizations and executing trades with certain races.
    *   **Promotion:** Required to **Promote kittens**, which increases their rank and production bonuses.
    *   [Construction:]( {{< relref "techs/Construction.md" >}}) A mandatory material for building [Temples]( {{< relref "buildings/Temple.md" >}}), [Tradeposts]( {{< relref "buildings/Tradepost.md" >}}), and [Mints]( {{< relref "buildings/Mint.md" >}}).
    *   **Religion:** Essential for purchasing high-tier **Order of the Sun** upgrades and executing **faith resets** via Apocrypha.

### **Prestige Currencies (Resets)**
These are earned by resetting the game and provide permanent, stacking benefits for future runs.
*   **Karma:** Earned if you have **more than 35 kittens** at reset. It primarily increases **Kitten Happiness**, which provides a global production bonus.
*   [Paragon:]( {{< relref "resource/paragon.md" >}}) Earned if you have **more than 70 kittens** at reset (1 point for every kitten over 70). It provides a **global production bonus** and increases **resource storage caps**.
*   **Burned Paragon:** Regular Paragon can be "burned" to provide a smaller but separate production boost that is highly effective during the [Dark Future]( {{< relref "guides/Dark_Future.md" >}}) (Year 40,000+).

### **Special and Late-Game Currencies**
*   **Catpower:** While technically a resource, it functions as a currency for **Sending Explorers** (1,000 Catpower) and initiating **Hunts** (100 Catpower).
*   **Blackcoin:** A speculative cryptocurrency bought and sold from [Leviathans]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Leviathans.md" >}}) in exchange for [relics]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}). Its price fluctuates over time.
*   [Time Crystals:]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) A rare currency used to purchase advanced temporal technologies like [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}) and to skip years via **time shattering**.
