[Chemistry]( {{< relref "techs/Chemistry.md" >}}) is a pivotal mid-game science technology that initiates the "Alloy Era", providing the necessary foundation for advanced industrialisation and early space exploration.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To research Chemistry, you must first unlock [Physics]( {{< relref "techs/Physics.md" >}}). The technology itself requires the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 60,000
*   [Compendium:]( {{< relref "resource/Compendium.md" >}}) 50

### **Unlocked Buildings and Resources**
Chemistry unlocks the ability to produce [Alloy]( {{< relref "craft/Alloy.md" >}}), a high-end craftable material made from Steel and Titanium. It also enables the construction of two critical industrial buildings:
*   [Oil Well:]( {{< relref "buildings/Oil_Well.md" >}}) Your primary source of terrestrial oil production, providing +0.02 oil per tick.
*   [Calciner:]( {{< relref "buildings/Calciner.md" >}}) A complex industrial structure that consumes minerals and oil to produce iron and small amounts of [Titanium]( {{< relref "resource/titanium.md" >}}). While slow, Calciners provide a steady domestic alternative to trading with Zebras for Titanium.

### **Workshop Upgrades**
Researching Chemistry makes several high-impact **Alloy-tier** upgrades available in the Workshop:
*   [Alloy Barns:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Barns.md" >}}) Increases the storage capacity of Barns, Warehouses, and Harbours for wood, minerals, and iron by **100%**.
*   [Alloy Warehouses:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Warehouses.md" >}}) Boosts the storage of wood, minerals, coal, iron, gold, and titanium in your facilities by **45%**.
*   [Alloy Axe:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Axe.md" >}}) Makes Woodcutters **50%** more effective.
*   [Alloy Armour:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Armour.md" >}}) Provides a **+0.5 hunting bonus**, making each hunt yield roughly 40–50% more furs and ivory.

### **Strategic Importance**
*   **Breaking Resource Caps:** The Alloy-tier storage upgrades are essential for reaching the high science and resource limits required for the **Concrete Era** and [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}).
*   **Transition to Space:** Chemistry is a mandatory step in the technology tree leading toward [Electricity]( {{< relref "techs/Electricity.md" >}}), [Biology]( {{< relref "techs/Biology.md" >}}), and eventually the [Rocketry]( {{< relref "techs/Rocketry.md" >}}) needed for space missions.
*   **Titanium Management:** Strategic guides recommend building several Calciners immediately after researching Chemistry to begin accumulating the Titanium needed for advanced Workshop upgrades before domestic production becomes more efficient through later Metallurgy upgrades.
*   **Village Progression:** Players typically aim to acquire all Alloy buildings when they have approximately **90–100 kittens**, using the expanded storage to push toward a population of 130+ for an efficient first reset.
