[Dimensional Physics]( {{< relref "techs/Dimensional_Physics.md" >}}) is a high-level late-game technology in *Kittens Game* that focuses on expanding resource storage and scientific capacity through the enhancement of [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}). It is part of the progression path toward time manipulation and antimatter technologies.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 235,000.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Particle Physics]( {{< relref "techs/Particle_Physics.md" >}}).
*   **Technology Path:** It is unlocked following [Nuclear Fission]( {{< relref "techs/Nuclear_Fission.md" >}}) and Particle Physics.

### **Unlocked Workshop Upgrades**
Researching Dimensional Physics is primarily done to access two powerful upgrades for the Accelerator building:

*   [Energy Rifts:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Energy_Rifts.md" >}})
    *   **Cost:** 200k Science, 7,500 Titanium, and 250 Uranium.
    *   **Effect:** Transforms Accelerators into storage facilities by creating "rifts to pocket dimensions".
    *   **Capacity Bonuses:** Each Accelerator provides substantial storage for several resources: **+30,000 Catnip**, **+20,000 Wood**, **+25,000 Minerals**, **+7,500 Iron**, **+2,500 Coal**, **+250 Gold**, and **+750 Titanium**.
    *   **Strategic Value:** This is a major late-game tool for bypassing the storage bottlenecks of standard buildings like Barns and Warehouses.

*   **LHC (Large Hadron Collider):**
    *   **Cost:** 250k Science, 100 Unobtainium, and 150 Alloy.
    *   **Effect:** Adds a flat bonus to your maximum science capacity for every Accelerator owned.
    *   **Bonus:** **+2,500 Max Science per Accelerator**.
    *   **Strategic Value:** This helps reach the massive science thresholds required for deep-space and temporal technologies, such as [Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}) and [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}).

### **Strategic Utility**
Dimensional Physics is often described as a turning point for resource management. By investing in [Energy Rifts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Energy_Rifts.md" >}}), players can significantly increase their **Titanium and Gold caps**, which are essential for mass trading with civilisations like the Zebras or Nagas. Furthermore, the [LHC]( {{< relref "upgrade/LHC.md" >}}) provides a scalable way to increase science storage without relying solely on expensive Observatories or Bio Labs.
