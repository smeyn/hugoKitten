[Hydroponics]( {{< relref "techs/Hydroponics.md" >}}) is an advanced, late-game space structure located on the [Yarn]( {{< relref "planet/Yarn.md" >}}) planet in the Space tab. It serves as a powerful tool for scaling your food economy to support massive populations without the risk of starvation.

### **Requirements and Costs**
Unlocking and building Hydroponics represents a significant technological investment:
*   **Technology:** You must first research [Terraformation]( {{< relref "techs/Terraformation.md" >}}) (750k Science, 5 Relics) to unlock the **Hydroponics technology**, which itself costs **1M Science and 25 Relics**.
*   **Mission:** You must complete the [Yarn Mission]( {{< relref "space/Yarn_Mission.md" >}}) (7,500 Starcharts, 35k Titanium, 350k Science, 12k Kerosene) to access the planet.
*   **Building Cost:** Each Hydroponics unit costs **1 Unobtainium and 500 Kerosene**.

### **Core Effects**
Hydroponics provides three distinct types of bonuses that help manage late-game resource bottlenecks:
*   **Catnip Production:** Each unit increases global catnip production by **+2.5%**.
*   **Catnip Storage:** Each unit provides a **+10% bonus** to your maximum catnip storage capacity. This bonus is additive with the [Refrigeration]( {{< relref "upgrade/Refrigeration.md" >}}) upgrade (+75%). For example, having 50 Hydroponics alongside Refrigeration results in a **+575%** total storage bonus.
*   **Terraforming Synergy:** Every unit provides a **+1.0% bonus** to the kitten capacity of **Terraforming Stations** (which normally house +1 kitten each).

### **Cycle Multipliers (Numerology)**
If you have researched the **Numerology** metaphysics upgrade, the effectiveness of Hydroponics is dramatically altered by the current **Cycle**:
*   [Yarn Cycle:]( {{< relref "Cycles/yarn_Cycle.md" >}}) Production effectiveness is increased by **+100%** (doubled).
*   [Piscine Cycle:]( {{< relref "Cycles/Piscine_Cycle.md" >}}) Production effectiveness is decreased by **-50%**.

### **Strategic Utility**
Hydroponics is primarily used to mitigate the **"happiness liability"** of a mature civilization. In the late game, high happiness (often over 400%) can cause kittens to consume more than triple their base catnip requirement. Because Hydroponics scales both **production and storage** multiplicatively with other bonuses, it allows you to maintain a high-happiness, high-productivity workforce without needing to dedicate a large portion of your population to Farmer jobs.
