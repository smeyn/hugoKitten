[Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}) is a high-level science technology that marks a major turning point in the late game, transitioning your civilization from standard industrialization into the era of time manipulation and void exploration. It is a mandatory step for unlocking the most advanced endgame mechanics.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
Researching Tachyon Theory is expensive and requires resources that can only be obtained through advanced trading or religious sacrifices.
*   **Science Cost:** 750,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 25 [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) and 1 [Relic]( {{< relref "resource/relic.md" >}}).
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}).

### **Unlocked Technology and Upgrades**
Researching this technology unlocks the [Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}) science and provides access to three powerful Workshop upgrades:

*   [Chronoforge:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoforge.md" >}}) Unlocks the "Chronoforge" section in the Time tab. This allows you to **combust (shatter) Time Crystals** to skip ahead to the next spring, advancing the calendar year instantly. This is the foundation of the late-game "Shatter Engine," used to rapidly generate antimatter and resources.
*   [Tachyon Accelerators:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Tachyon_Accelerators.md" >}}) A massive storage upgrade for [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}), increasing the capacity they provide for catnip, wood, minerals, iron, coal, gold, and titanium by **500%**.
*   [Chronoengineers:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoengineers.md" >}}) An efficiency upgrade that allows [Engineers]( {{< relref "job/Engineer.md" >}}) to continue performing crafts during a time skip (provided you have Resource Retrieval levels).

### **Strategic Importance**
Tachyon Theory is primarily used as a gateway to the **Endgame Reset Loop**. By unlocking the Chronoforge, it allows you to manipulate time to stay within beneficial cycles, such as the [Redmoon cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Redmoon_cycle.md" >}}), which provides a massive boost to [Unobtainium]( {{< relref "resource/unobtainium.md" >}}) production. 

Furthermore, it is a required prerequisite for [Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}), which is necessary to attempt the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}) and to begin collecting [Void]( {{< relref "resource/void.md" >}}), a resource essential for reaching the game’s theoretical limits.
