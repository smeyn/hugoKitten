[Artificial Intelligence]( {{< relref "techs/Artificial_Intelligence.md" >}}) is an advanced late-game science that serves as the gateway to quantum computing and high-tier automation. It is researched in the Science tab for **250,000 Science and 150 Blueprints**.

### **The AI Core and Gigaflops**
Researching this technology unlocks the [AI Core]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}) building, a megastructure that produces **Gigaflops**.
*   **Production:** Each AI Core generates **+0.02 Gigaflops per tick**.
*   **Energy Consumption:** AI Cores are power-intensive, consuming energy according to the formula: $2 + (1.5 \times \text{number of AI Cores})$.
*   **Risks (The AI Apocalypse):** Gigaflops represent raw processing power that increases your "AI Level." If your AI Level reaches **15**, an **AI Apocalypse** occurs, causing you to start losing resources. Resetting the game is the only way to end an active AI Apocalypse and clear accumulated Gigaflops.

### **Workshop Upgrades and Synergies**
Despite the risks, Artificial Intelligence provides critical upgrades that scale ground and space infrastructure:
*   [Machine Learning:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Machine_Learning.md" >}}) Increases the effectiveness of [Data Centers]( {{< relref "buildings/Data_Center.md" >}}) by **+10% for every AI Core** you own.
*   [AI Bases:]( {{< relref "upgrade/AI_Bases.md" >}}) Enhances **Moon Base** storage capacity by **+10% per AI Core**.
*   **Neural Network:** A powerful efficiency upgrade that **doubles the effectiveness of all Engineers**.
*   [AI Engineers:]( {{< relref "upgrade/AI_Engineers.md" >}}) Significantly improves the "know-how" of Engineers, allowing them to craft more resources per tick.

### **Strategic Management**
To benefit from AI Cores without triggering an apocalypse, players must balance them with **Entanglement Stations** (unlocked via Quantum Cryptography).
*   **Hash Production:** Entanglement Stations consume **0.1 Gigaflops per tick** to produce **Hashes**, which are used to reverse-engineer relics.
*   **The Safe Ratio:** It is generally recommended to maintain a ratio of roughly **5 AI Cores to 1 Entanglement Station** to ensure Gigaflops are consumed as fast as they are produced, keeping the AI Level stable.
*   **Transkittenism Policy:** This policy allows your society to "merge with the AI." It **doubles Gigaflop production**, prevents the AI Apocalypse from occurring, and strengthens both AI Bases and Machine Learning.
