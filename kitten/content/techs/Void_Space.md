[Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}) is a high-tier endgame science technology and a dedicated section in the **Time tab** that marks the transition into Phase 3 of *Kittens Game*, focusing on advanced temporal mechanics and the use of the [Void]( {{< relref "resource/void.md" >}}) resource.

### **Technological Unlock**
*   **Cost:** 800,000 Science, 30 Time Crystals, and 100 Void.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}).
*   **Immediate Unlocks:** Researching this technology grants access to the [Atheism challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}), the [Paradox Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Paradox_Theory.md" >}}) science, and the **Cryochambers** and [Void Aspiration]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Aspiration.md" >}}) workshop upgrades.

### **The Atheism Challenge**
Void Space is the mandatory requirement to attempt the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}). In this challenge, the **Order of the Sun** is unavailable, meaning you must play without any faith bonuses. The goal is to reset the game with at least one **Cryochamber** constructed.

### **Void Space Structures (Time Tab)**
Once the technology is researched, several advanced structures become available in the Void Space section of the Time tab:
*   **Cryochambers:** Each chamber houses one kitten and allows you to carry that kitten (and its experience) over to your next game. You cannot build more cryochambers than your current number of active [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}).
*   **Void Hoover:** Unlocked via Void Aspiration; increases the maximum amount of void you can gain during a [Temporal Paradox]( {{< relref "guides/temporal_paradox.md" >}}) day by 1.
*   **Void Rift:** Unlocked via Void Aspiration; each rift increases your **global resource storage ratio by 2%** and boosts **HR Harvester** energy production by 10%.
*   **Chronocontrol:** Unlocked via Paradox Theory; increases the number of days a Temporal Paradox lasts.
*   **Void Resonator:** Unlocked via Paradox Theory; improves faith production and allows **Order of the Void** to trigger during a time skip.

### **Strategic Importance**
Void Space is a critical gatekeeper for the **Endgame Reset Loop**. It is the prerequisite for [Paradox Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Paradox_Theory.md" >}}), which unlocks **Resource Retrieval**—the mechanic that allows you to gain a percentage of your yearly resources every time you shatter a Time Crystal. Furthermore, building a "Void Seed" through numerous **Void Rifts** is a primary strategy for reaching the massive **Unobtainium caps** (over 8 billion) required for self-sustaining positive resets in the late game.
