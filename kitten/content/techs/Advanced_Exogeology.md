[Advanced Exogeology]( {{< relref "techs/Advanced_Exogeology.md" >}}), now primarily referred to as **Exometallurgy**, is an essential late-game science technology that serves as the gateway to the **Eludium Era**. It represents a significant shift in production scaling, focusing on the refinement of space-based resources and the drastic expansion of population and scientific limits.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 325,000.
*   **Blueprint Cost:** 350 Blueprints.
*   **Prerequisite:** Researching [Exogeology]( {{< relref "resource/exogeology.md" >}}) is mandatory before this technology becomes available.

### **Unlocking Eludium**
The primary function of this technology is enabling the crafting of [Eludium]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}) in the Workshop. Eludium is an extremely rare and expensive composite resource required for deep-space missions and advanced storage structures.
*   **Crafting Recipe:** 1,000 Unobtainium and 2,500 Alloy.

### **Enabled Workshop Upgrades**
Researching Advanced Exogeology/Exometallurgy provides access to four high-impact Workshop upgrades that optimize the late-game economy:

*   [Microwarp Reactors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Microwarp_Reactors.md" >}})
    *   **Cost:** 150k Science and 50 Eludium.
    *   **Effect:** Increases the **Unobtainium production of Lunar Outposts by 75%**. This is considered a vital upgrade for scaling UO production to meet endgame demands.
*   [Eludium Huts:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Eludium_Huts.md" >}})
    *   **Cost:** 275k Science and 125 Eludium.
    *   **Effect:** Reduces the **Hut price ratio by 10%**. This reduction allows for significantly higher kitten populations, which is critical for maximizing Paragon gains upon resetting.
*   [Planet Busters:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Planet_Busters.md" >}})
    *   **Cost:** 275k Science and 250 Eludium.
    *   **Effect:** Doubles the effectiveness of [Planet Crackers]( {{< relref "space/planet_cracker.md" >}}) (Hissmeowra), making them twice as efficient at producing Uranium.
*   [Eludium Reflectors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Eludium_Reflectors.md" >}})
    *   **Cost:** 250k Science and 15 Eludium.
    *   **Effect:** Improves the maximum science bonus provided by **Observatories to Libraries and Data Centers by an additional 2%** each.

### **Strategic Utility**
Advanced Exogeology is the threshold where [Unobtainium]( {{< relref "resource/unobtainium.md" >}}) transitions from a rare mission requirement to a primary industrial input. The technology and its associated upgrades are designed to overcome the production bottlenecks of the space era. Specifically, [Microwarp Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Microwarp_Reactors.md" >}}) are a priority for any run lasting longer than five minutes, as they provide a massive boost to UO velocity. While [Eludium Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Eludium_Huts.md" >}}) are expensive, they are essential for players pushing toward the highest possible population caps (such as 500+ kittens) to maximize the return on investment for each reset.
