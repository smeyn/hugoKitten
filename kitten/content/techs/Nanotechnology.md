[Nanotechnology]( {{< relref "techs/Nanotechnology.md" >}}) is a high-level science in the late-mid game that serves as a vital gateway to endgame industrial efficiency and space exploration. It is unlocked after researching [Nuclear Fission]( {{< relref "techs/Nuclear_Fission.md" >}}) and requires **200,000 Science and 150 Blueprints** to research.

### **Unlocked Workshop Upgrades**
Researching Nanotechnology provides access to several powerful upgrades that drastically scale your production and efficiency:

*   [Augmentations:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Augmentations.md" >}}) This is one of the most significant upgrades for population efficiency, as it increases the production bonus from kitten skills by **100%**. This stacks with other multipliers to make your specialized workforce far more productive.
*   [Nanosuits:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Nanosuits.md" >}}) This upgrade provides a flat **+0.5 hunting bonus**, increasing the yield of rare resources like Furs and Ivory from hunts. Notably, the **Manager** kitten trait provides a bonus equivalent to this upgrade.
*   [Photovoltaic Cells:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Photovoltaic_Cells.md" >}}) This significantly boosts your renewable energy infrastructure by making **Solar Farms 50% more effective** at producing energy.
*   [Fluidized Reactors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Fluidized_Reactors.md" >}}) This dramatically improves the output of your [Calciners]( {{< relref "buildings/Calciner.md" >}}), causing them to produce **+100% more Iron** and **+300% more Titanium**.

### **Role in Scientific Progression**
Nanotechnology is a mandatory prerequisite for the following advanced technologies:
*   [Superconductors:]( {{< relref "techs/Superconductors.md" >}}) The next step in the physics tree, which eventually leads to [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}).
*   [Particle Physics:]( {{< relref "techs/Particle_Physics.md" >}}) This unlocks [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}), which are essential for generating [Uranium]( {{< relref "resource/uranium.md" >}}) and expanding resource storage.

### **Space and Industrial Utility**
In addition to workshop upgrades, Nanotechnology is a required component for the [Space Elevator]( {{< relref "space/space_elevator.md" >}}) (when combined with Orbital Engineering). The Space Elevator is crucial for the space race as it **reduces the oil requirements** for space missions and structures by up to 75% through diminishing returns.
