[Satellites]( {{< relref "techs/Satellites.md" >}}) are essential space structures in *Kittens Game* that bridge the gap between early-game astronomy and advanced interstellar exploration. They are primarily used to automate starchart production and significantly expand your scientific capacity.

### **Unlocking and Deployment**
*   **Prerequisites:** You must first research [Rocketry]( {{< relref "techs/Rocketry.md" >}}) to unlock the **Satellites technology** (costing 190k Science and 125 Blueprints). Once the technology is researched, the "Deploy Satellite" mission becomes available in the Space tab.
*   **Deployment Cost:** Each satellite costs **325 Starcharts**, **2,500 Titanium**, **100k Science**, and **15k Oil**. 
    *   *Note:* In the **Black Sky challenge**, the cost is altered to remove the starchart requirement, making them accessible without prior astronomical observations.

### **Core Effects**
Deploying satellites provides immediate global benefits to your science and resource generation:
*   **Science Cap:** Each satellite improves the **Science capacity of Observatories by 5%**. This bonus can be further increased by 10% if the [Militarize Space]( {{< relref "Policy/Militarize_Space.md" >}}) policy is enacted.
*   **Starchart Production:** Satellites generate a base of **0.005 starcharts per second**. While slower than Research Vessels, they are often the first automated source of starcharts available to the player.
*   **Energy Consumption:** By default, each satellite **consumes 1 Watt** of energy.

### **Workshop Upgrades and Synergies**
The utility of satellites is greatly enhanced through various Workshop upgrades:
*   [Hubble Space Telescope:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Hubble_Space_Telescope.md" >}}) Increases the starchart production of all buildings, including satellites, by **30%**.
*   [Solar Satellites:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Solar_Satellites.md" >}}) Transforms satellites from energy consumers into producers, causing each one to **generate 1 Watt** instead of consuming it.
*   [Satellite Navigation:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Satellite_Navigation.md" >}}) This is a critical mid-game upgrade where every active satellite reduces the starchart cost of crafting [Trade Ships]( {{< relref "craft/Trade_Ship.md" >}}) by **1.25%** (capped at a 75% discount).
*   [Satellite Radio:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Satellite_Radio.md" >}}) Every active satellite provides a **+0.5% bonus** to the maximum culture and culture production of [Broadcast Towers]( {{< relref "buildings/Broadcast_Tower.md" >}}).

### **Cyclical and Environmental Impacts**
The effectiveness of your satellite network fluctuates based on the current temporal cycle:
*   [Cath Cycle:]( {{< relref "Cycles/Cath_Cycle.md" >}}) Provides a **+100% bonus** to satellite starchart production and the science bonus they provide to observatories.
*   [Kairo Cycle:]( {{< relref "Cycles/Kairo_Cycle.md" >}}) Imposes a **-25% penalty** to these same bonuses.
*   **Challenges:** Building at least one satellite is a mandatory goal for completing the **Energy challenge**. In the **Black Sky challenge**, their starchart production is reduced based on the number of times you have completed the challenge.
