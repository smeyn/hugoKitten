[Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}) is a pivotal mid-to-late game technology that marks the transition from simple space exploration to the manipulation of time itself. It is the primary gateway to carrying resources over through resets and unlocking the advanced "Void" and "Relic" phases of the game.

### **Requirements and Costs**
Chronophysics is often the first technology players encounter that requires [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) rather than standard resources.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Particle Physics]( {{< relref "techs/Particle_Physics.md" >}}).
*   **Science Cost:** 250,000.
*   **Special Cost:** 5 Time Crystals.

*Strategic Note:* For your first run, obtaining these 5 Time Crystals usually requires trading **5,000 Unobtainium** with the Leviathans or sacrificing **25 Alicorns** at the Ziggurat.

### **Key Unlocks**
Researching Chronophysics unlocks three critical components for endgame progression:

| Unlock | Function |
| :--- | :--- |
| [Chronosphere]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}) | A mega-structure that allows you to **carry over resources** to a new reset. Each sphere preserves 1.5% of your non-crafted resources. |
| [Flux Condensator]( {{< relref "upgrade/Flux_Condensator.md" >}}) | A Workshop upgrade that allows Chronospheres to also preserve **crafted resources** (like Blueprints, Eludium, and Steel). |
| [Stasis Chambers]( {{< relref "upgrade/Stasis_Chambers.md" >}}) | An upgrade that nearly doubles the storage capacity of your [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}). |
| [Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}) | The subsequent technology required to unlock the [Chronoforge]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoforge.md" >}}), [Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}), and the [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}). |

### **Synergy with Anachronomancy**
One of the most powerful interactions in the game involves the [Anachronomancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Anachronomancy.md" >}}) metaphysics upgrade.
*   **Permanent Unlock:** If you own Anachronomancy, [Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}) is saved across resets.
*   **Immediate Access:** This means you start every future run with Chronophysics already researched. Combined with carried-over Time Crystals and Unobtainium, this allows you to build Chronospheres immediately at the start of a new run, drastically accelerating your recovery speed.

### **Strategic Context**
You previously asked about [resets]( {{< relref "guides/Reset.md" >}}) and **Iron Will**. Chronophysics is the technology that makes resetting highly profitable rather than a complete restart. By building Chronospheres (unlocked here) and the Flux Condensator, you can carry over millions of blueprints and science resources, effectively bypassing the early-game "grind" entirely.
