[Terraformation]( {{< relref "techs/Terraformation.md" >}}) is a high-level endgame science technology in *Kittens Game* that serves as a vital gateway to scaling your population and energy production through deep-space engineering. 

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 750,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 5 Relics.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}).

### **Unlocked Technologies and Structures**
Researching Terraformation is required to unlock several critical endgame components:
*   **Hydroponics (Technology):** Unlocks the Hydroponics structure, which is mandatory for massive late-game food scaling.
*   **Terraforming Station (Building):** Located on the planet [Yarn]( {{< relref "planet/Yarn.md" >}}) in the Space tab.
*   **Tectonic (Building):** Located in the [Centaurus System]( {{< relref "planet/Centaurus_System.md" >}}) in the Space tab.

### **The Terraforming Station (Yarn)**
This structure represents the process of melting Yarn's ice and oxygenating its atmosphere to support life. 
*   **Cost:** 25 Antimatter, 5,000 Uranium, and 5,000 Kerosene.
*   **Effect:** Each station increases your maximum kitten population by **+1**.
*   **Synergy:** Every unit of [Hydroponics]( {{< relref "techs/Hydroponics.md" >}}) you own provides a **+1.0% bonus** to the kitten capacity of your Terraforming Stations, allowing them to eventually house more than one kitten each.

### **Tectonic (Centaurus System)**
This structure involves ripping open the planet near the star Centaurus to harvest magma energy.
*   **Cost:** 600,000 Science, 500 Antimatter, and 75,000 Thorium.
*   **Effect:** Each Tectonic provides **+25 Energy**.
*   **Upgrades:** Every level of **Molten Core** (requires Exophysics) improves Tectonic energy production by an additional **5%**.
*   **Challenge Goal:** Building at least one Tectonic is a requirement for completing the **Energy Challenge**.

### **Strategic Utility**
Terraformation is a cornerstone of the **Endgame Paragon** phase. By "Terraforming Yarn," players can build a vast amount of housing (potentially support up to 60,000 kittens), which is essential for generating massive amounts of Paragon per reset. Additionally, Tectonic structures provide the high-density energy required to power late-game facilities like [AI Cores]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}) and **Entanglement Stations** without relying solely on ground-based reactors.
