[Superconductors]( {{< relref "techs/Superconductors.md" >}}) is an advanced late-mid-game technology that acts as a vital transition point between industrial expansion and deep-space exploration. It focuses on enhancing energy efficiency and bridging the gap to high-tier temporal and antimatter mechanics.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 225,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 175 Blueprints.
*   **Prerequisite:** Researching [Nanotechnology]( {{< relref "techs/Nanotechnology.md" >}}) is required before this science becomes available.

### **Unlocked Technology**
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) Superconductors is the mandatory prerequisite for the [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) technology, which begins Phase 4 of the game (Chrono-Optimization and Geometric Growth).

### **Unlocked Workshop Upgrades**
Researching this technology provides access to three highly impactful upgrades that drastically improve your energy balance and production:

*   [Space Manufacturing:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Space_Manufacturing.md" >}})
    *   **Effect:** This allows your village's **Factories** to provide a production bonus to space-based structures based on 75% of your factory craft ratio. 
    *   **Impact:** It specifically increases the Space Bonus from [Space Elevators]( {{< relref "space/space_elevator.md" >}}) and [Orbital Arrays]( {{< relref "space/orbital_array.md" >}}) by **3.75% per factory** (this increases to 4.5% if you also have the Factory Logistics upgrade). This is essential for scaling [Unobtainium]( {{< relref "resource/unobtainium.md" >}}) production to the levels required for the deep endgame.
*   [Cold Fusion:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cold_Fusion.md" >}})
    *   **Effect:** Increases the energy output of **Reactors by 25%**. 
    *   **Impact:** This provides a flat boost of **+2.5 Watts** per active Reactor, significantly easing energy bottlenecks.
*   [Cryocomputing:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cryocomputing.md" >}})
    *   **Effect:** Reduces the energy consumption of **Data Centers by 50%**. 
    *   **Impact:** Each Data Center's power drain is reduced from **2 Watts to 1 Watt**, allowing you to maintain massive science storage without overwhelming your power grid.

### **Strategic Importance**
Superconductors is often considered the threshold for the **"Positive Reset" loop**. By unlocking [Space Manufacturing]( {{< relref "upgrade/Space_Manufacturing.md" >}}), players can finally farm enough Unobtainium to build the high number of [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}) needed to carry over more resources than they spent during a run. Additionally, the combined effects of [Cold Fusion]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cold_Fusion.md" >}}) and [Cryocomputing]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cryocomputing.md" >}}) allow for a much larger industrial and scientific infrastructure to be powered simultaneously, which is critical for supporting energy-intensive late-game buildings like [AI Cores]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}) and **Entanglement Stations**.
