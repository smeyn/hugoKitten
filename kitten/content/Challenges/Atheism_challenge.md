The [Atheism Challenge]( {{< relref "Challenges/Atheism_challenge.md" >}}) is a significant milestone within the "Special Modes" of *Kittens Game*, designed to bridge the gap between standard play and the advanced **Relic Era**. It is unlocked through the [Adjustment Bureau]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Adjustment_Bureau.md" >}}) metaphysics upgrade.

### **The Atheism Challenge Overview**
The primary restriction of this mode is the complete removal of the **Order of the Sun**, which means players receive **no faith-based production bonus** (Solar Revolution) throughout the run. 

*   **Goal:** Successfully reset the game while owning at least one **Cryochamber**.
*   **Infrastructure Requirements:** To reach the goal, you must research [Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}), [Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}), and [Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}). A single cryochamber costs 100 Void, 2 Time Crystals, and 1 Karma.
*   **Preparation:** It is highly recommended to enter the challenge with a minimum of **63 Time Crystals and 1 Relic** to ensure you can afford the necessary late-game research and structures without the help of a faith bonus.

### **Atheism in the Context of Special Modes**
Challenges in *Kittens Game* are "Special Modes" that impose specific constraints in exchange for permanent, stacking rewards. 

*   **Negative Synergies:** Combining Atheism with other challenges often creates severe bottlenecks. For example, running **Atheism and Unicorn Tears** simultaneously makes generating unicorn resources nearly impossible, as the faith bonus is one of the few multipliers affecting them.
*   **Resource Management:** In most challenges, including Atheism, [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}) do not bring resources into the run. Instead, those resources are placed in **reserves** and can only be reclaimed after the challenge is completed.
*   **Iron Will Interaction:** While **Iron Will** (the kittenless mode) is technically a "Special Mode," it does not require the Adjustment Bureau to unlock. Attempting Atheism within an Iron Will run is exceptionally difficult due to the lack of worker-based production and faith multipliers.

### **Strategic Rewards**
The rewards for completing Atheism focus on removing the standard caps on faith-based production, making it essential for late-game optimisation:

| Reward Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Base Reward** | The **Solar Revolution cap** increases by **100 percentage points per Transcendence tier**. This is equivalent to owning 20 Black Obelisks. |
| **Increasing Reward** | Each subsequent completion increases **Solar Revolution effectiveness by 10%** (capped at 400%). This effect is multiplicative with [Black Obelisks]( {{< relref "transcendence/Black_Obelisk.md" >}}). |

Completion of the challenge also allows for the construction of more **cryochambers** in future runs, provided you have the support of enough Chronospheres. This is vital for carrying over a larger population of skilled kittens into new timelines.
