[Gears]( {{< relref "craft/Gear.md" >}}) are a critical mid-to-late-game craftable resource in *Kittens Game*, functioning as an integral component for buildings and upgrades that increase **automation** and industrial efficiency.

### **Production and Acquisition**
*   **Recipe:** One Gear is crafted in the Workshop using **15 Steel**.
*   **Prerequisites:** You must first research the [Steel]( {{< relref "craft/Steel.md" >}}) science technology to unlock Gear crafting.
*   **Crafting Efficiency:** Kittens with the **Metallurgist** trait provide a specific **+0.1 bonus** to your `craftRatio` for Gears when assigned as the village leader.

### **Primary Building Requirements**
Gears are a mandatory material for constructing several industrial and resource-gathering structures:
*   [Steamworks:]( {{< relref "buildings/Steamworks.md" >}}) Requires **20 Gears**.
*   [Magnetos:]( {{< relref "buildings/Magneto.md" >}}) Requires **5 Gears**.
*   [Oil Wells:]( {{< relref "buildings/Oil_Well.md" >}}) Requires **25 Gears**.

### **Key Workshop Upgrades**
Gears are heavily utilized in the Workshop, particularly for upgrades that automate manual tasks or enhance industrial output:
*   **Printing & Pressing:** Upgrades like the [Printing Press]( {{< relref "upgrade/Printing_Press.md" >}}) (45 Gears), [Offset Press]( {{< relref "upgrade/Offset_Press.md" >}}) (250 Gears), and [Photolithography]( {{< relref "upgrade/Photolithography.md" >}}) (1,250 Gears) allow Steamworks to automatically generate manuscripts.
*   **Industrial Automation:** [Workshop Automation]( {{< relref "upgrade/Workshop_Automation.md" >}}) (25 Gears) and [Advanced Automation]( {{< relref "upgrade/Advanced_Automation.md" >}}) (75 Gears) enable Steamworks to automatically craft slabs and beams.
*   **Production Boosts:** Upgrades such as [Steel Plants]( {{< relref "upgrade/Steel_Plants.md" >}}) (750 Gears) and [Rotary Kiln]( {{< relref "upgrade/Rotary_Kiln.md" >}}) (500 Gears) improve the efficiency of Calciners.
*   **Environmental & Efficiency:** [Carbon Sequestration]( {{< relref "upgrade/Carbon_Sequestration.md" >}}) (125 Gears) reduces factory pollution, while [Robotic Assistance]( {{< relref "upgrade/Robotic_Assistance.md" >}}) (10,000 Gears) significantly reduces kitten catnip consumption.

### **Strategic Importance**
Gears represent the transition from manual resource management to **automated industrial cycles**. Because Gears are crafted from Steel (which is itself a refined resource), it is highly recommended to **maximize your Workshops** and Factories to achieve a high craft ratio. This ensures you produce multiple Gears for the same amount of Steel, which is vital for overcoming the "Theology hurdle" and establishing a robust industrial base.
