A [Compendium]( {{< relref "resource/Compendium.md" >}}) is a mid-game craftable resource that represents the "sum of all modern knowledge of catkind". It is primarily used to expand your **Science storage capacity**, which is essential for unlocking late-game technologies and space missions.

### **Production and Costs**
To craft a Compendium, you must first research the [Philosophy]( {{< relref "techs/Philosophy.md" >}}) technology.

*   **Cost:** 50 Manuscripts and 10,000 Science.
*   **Crafting Efficiency:** Like other workshop resources, the number of Compendiums produced per craft is significantly increased by your [Workshop]( {{< relref "buildings/Workshop.md" >}}) and [Factory]( {{< relref "buildings/Factory.md" >}}) craft bonuses.
*   **Metaphysics Bonus:** The **Codex Logos** upgrade (50 Paragon) adds +25% to the craft ratio and provides a x1.1 multiplier to Compendium crafting.

### **Primary Effects**
*   **Science Storage:** Each Compendium provides a permanent **+10 bonus to your maximum Science cap**.
*   **Storage Cap:** By default, the maximum bonus you can receive from Compendiums is capped at 100% of your total science storage from buildings. 
    *   In **Iron Will** mode, this cap is significantly higher (1000%).
    *   **Data Centres** increase the "Compendium Cap" by 1,000 each.
    *   The [Uplink]( {{< relref "upgrade/Uplink.md" >}}) and [Starlink]( {{< relref "upgrade/Starlink.md" >}}) workshop upgrades further increase this cap by +1% per [Bio Lab]( {{< relref "buildings/Bio_Lab.md" >}}).

### **Strategic Utility**
Compendiums act as a bridge between the industrial era and the space era, where technology costs begin to exceed the storage limits of standard buildings.

*   **Technological Gatekeeper:** Compendiums are a required component for researching critical sciences, including:
    *   [Architecture]( {{< relref "techs/Architecture.md" >}}) (10 Compendiums)
    *   [Physics]( {{< relref "techs/Physics.md" >}}) (35 Compendiums)
    *   [Chemistry]( {{< relref "techs/Chemistry.md" >}}) (50 Compendiums)
    *   [Acoustics]( {{< relref "techs/Acoustics.md" >}}) (60 Compendiums)
    *   [Geology]( {{< relref "techs/Geology.md" >}}) (65 Compendiums)
    *   [Biology]( {{< relref "techs/Biology.md" >}}) (100 Compendiums)
*   **Grinding Blueprints:** Compendiums are the raw material for crafting [Blueprints]( {{< relref "resource/Blueprint.md" >}}) (25,000 Science + 25 Compendiums), which are necessary for advanced industrial and space structures.
*   **Chronosphere Resets:** When resetting with [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}), carrying over a large stockpile of Compendiums is vital. Because they preserve your science cap, you can immediately research expensive technologies in the next run without waiting to rebuild massive amounts of libraries and observatories.

### **Optimization Tip**
To grind Compendiums efficiently, focus on maximizing your [Culture]( {{< relref "resource/culture.md" >}}) and **Catpower** production first. This allows you to mass-produce [Manuscripts]( {{< relref "resource/manuscript.md" >}}), which are the primary bottleneck for Compendium crafting. Once your Science cap is full, use the "Craft All" function in the Workshop to convert those manuscripts into Compendiums.
