[Concrete]( {{< relref "craft/Concrete.md" >}}) is a critical mid-to-late-game craftable resource used primarily for advanced infrastructure, storage expansion, and space exploration. Its acquisition marks the beginning of the "Concrete Era," which is a major milestone in a player's first run.

### **Production and Unlocking**
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research the [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) science technology (115K Science, 45 Blueprints) to unlock the ability to craft concrete and assign [Engineers]( {{< relref "job/Engineer.md" >}}) to automate its production.
*   **Recipe:** Crafting one unit of concrete requires **2,500 Slabs** and **25 Steel**.
*   **Trait Bonus:** Kittens with the **Chemist** trait provide a specific bonus to crafting efficiency for concrete when assigned as a leader.

### **Major Upgrades**
Concrete is essential for several Workshop upgrades that significantly boost village capacity:
*   [Concrete Huts:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) Reduces the price ratio of Huts by **0.3 (30%)**, allowing for a much larger kitten population.
*   [Concrete Pillars:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}) Increases the maximum storage of wood, minerals, and iron in Barns and Warehouses by **5%**, and gold/titanium by **5%**.
*   [Concrete Barns:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}) Increases the storage capacity of Barns by **75%**.
*   [Concrete Warehouses:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Warehouses.md" >}}) Increases the storage capacity of Warehouses by **35%**.

### **Construction Uses**
Many advanced buildings and space structures require concrete as a primary material:
*   **Industrial/Science:** Factories (15), Reactors (50), Accelerators (125), Hydro Plants (100), and Data Centers (10).
*   **Space Infrastructure:** Lunar Outposts (150), Moon Bases (250), and Cryostations (1,500).

### **Strategic Importance**
The "Concrete Era" is often the target for a player's **first reset**. Strategy guides generally recommend pushing until you can afford [Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) and reach at least **130 kittens**. This ensures you gain at least **60 Paragon**, providing a substantial production and storage boost for your next run. To reach the 125K science cap required for Concrete Huts, players often research [Biology]( {{< relref "techs/Biology.md" >}}) to build [Bio Labs]( {{< relref "buildings/Bio_Lab.md" >}}).
