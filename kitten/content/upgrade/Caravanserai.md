[Caravanserai]( {{< relref "upgrade/Caravanserai.md" >}}) is a critical mid-game Workshop upgrade that enhances your trade efficiency by improving relations with other civilizations.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 25,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 10,000 Ivory and 250 Gold.
*   **Prerequisite Technology:** You must first research [Navigation]( {{< relref "techs/Navigation.md" >}}).

### **Core Effects and Mechanics**
The primary function of Caravanserai is to allow your [Tradeposts]( {{< relref "buildings/Tradepost.md" >}}) to influence race standing:
*   **Standing Bonus:** Once researched, every Tradepost you build provides a flat **+0.35% bonus to trade relations**.
*   **Hostility Mitigation:** This bonus manifests as a **0.35% decrease in the probability of a "hostile event"** (where a race gives you no resources) and a 0.175% increase in "friendly events" (yielding 15–25% extra resources).
*   **Synergy with Diplomacy:** If you have the [Diplomacy]( {{< relref "Policy/Diplomacy.md" >}}) metaphysics upgrade, the effectiveness of your Tradeposts in improving standing is further increased.

### **Strategic Importance**
The main purpose of Caravanserai is to manage **hostile races**, specifically the [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}), who are your primary source of Titanium. 
*   **Negating Hostility:** Without this upgrade, Zebras have a high chance to simply send your kittens packing with no resources.
*   **Efficiency Benchmarks:** With Caravanserai active, building **85 Tradeposts** will completely negate the Zebra's hostility, ensuring every trade is successful. If you also have the Diplomacy upgrade, this benchmark is reduced to only **58 Tradeposts**.
*   **Mid-Game Progression:** Strategic guides recommend acquiring Caravanserai as soon as you begin the "Titanium buildup" phase to avoid wasting precious gold and catpower on failed trades.
