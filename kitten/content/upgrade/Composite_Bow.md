The [Composite Bow]( {{< relref "upgrade/Composite_Bow.md" >}}) is an early-game **Workshop upgrade** that permanently increases the efficiency of your workforce's hunting capabilities.

### **Core Effects and Stats**
*   **Effect:** It provides a **+50% bonus** to **Catpower production** for all kittens assigned to the [Hunter]( {{< relref "job/Hunter.md" >}}) job.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research the [Construction]( {{< relref "techs/Construction.md" >}}) technology to unlock it.
*   **Cost:** 500 Science, 100 Iron, and 200 Wood.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Hunting Frequency:** By boosting the rate at which Catpower generates, the Composite Bow allows you to send **hunts** more frequently. This is critical in the early game for accumulating [Furs]( {{< relref "resource/Fur.md" >}}) (for Parchment) and [Ivory]( {{< relref "resource/ivory.md" >}}) (for happiness and trade).
*   **Weapon Progression:** It is the first tier of weapon upgrades, serving as a prerequisite step before you can eventually unlock better versions like the [Crossbow]( {{< relref "upgrade/Crossbow.md" >}}) (Machinery) and [Railgun]( {{< relref "upgrade/Railgun.md" >}}) (Particle Physics).

### **Relevance to Iron Will**
In this mode, [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}) replace kittens and act as your Hunters. Consequently, purchasing the Composite Bow will increase the passive Catpower generation of your Zebra population, allowing for more frequent trading and hunting without a kitten workforce.
