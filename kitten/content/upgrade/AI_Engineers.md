[AI Engineers]( {{< relref "upgrade/AI_Engineers.md" >}}) is a specialized late-game Workshop upgrade that provides the final set of specific "know-how" boosts to your [Engineer]( {{< relref "job/Engineer.md" >}}) kittens, significantly enhancing their automated crafting capabilities across all five mastery classes.

### **Acquisition and Costs**
To access this upgrade, you must first research the [Artificial Intelligence]( {{< relref "techs/Artificial_Intelligence.md" >}}) science technology (which requires 250,000 Science and 150 Blueprints). Once the technology is researched, the upgrade can be purchased in the Workshop for:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 35,000
*   [Eludium:]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}) 50
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) 500

### **Core Effects: Know-how Scaling**
The upgrade improves the base "know-how" of your Engineers, which serves as a multiplier for their crafting productivity. It provides the following specific values to each class:
*   **Class 1:** 10 know-how
*   **Class 2:** 5 know-how
*   **Class 3:** 5 know-how
*   **Class 4:** 2 know-how
*   **Class 5:** 2 know-how

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Final Know-how Tier:** This is the highest tier of specific class-based upgrades, following [Factory Optimization]( {{< relref "upgrade/Factory_Optimization.md" >}}), [Factory Robotics]( {{< relref "upgrade/Factory_Robotics.md" >}}), and [Space Engineers]( {{< relref "upgrade/Space_Engineers.md" >}}).
*   **Endgame Automation:** AI Engineers is critical for managing the high resource volumes of the late game. It ensures that your Engineers can convert raw materials into refined goods (like [Eludium]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}), [Alloy]( {{< relref "craft/Alloy.md" >}}), and [Kerosene]( {{< relref "resource/kerosene.md" >}})) fast enough to prevent them from hitting storage caps, especially during time skips.
*   **Synergy with Neural Network:** Researching AI Engineers is typically done in tandem with the **Neural Network** workshop upgrade (also unlocked by AI science). While AI Engineers increases base know-how, Neural Network **doubles the overall effectiveness of your Engineers**, creating a massive multiplicative boost to your village's automated industrial output.
