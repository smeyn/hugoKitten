[Catnip Enrichment]( {{< relref "upgrade/Catnip_Enrichment.md" >}}) is an early-game Workshop upgrade that significantly improves the efficiency of your wood production by doubling the effectiveness of catnip refinement.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock this upgrade, you must first research the [Construction]( {{< relref "techs/Construction.md" >}}) science technology (which costs 1,300 Science). Once researched, the upgrade becomes available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 500
*   **Catnip:** 5,000

### **Core Effect**
The primary function of Catnip Enrichment is to reduce the raw material cost of crafting wood:
*   **Resource Efficiency:** It reduces the cost to [Refine Catnip]( {{< relref "craft/Refine_Catnip.md" >}}) into wood by 50%.
*   **Refinement Ratio:** Without this upgrade, crafting 1 unit of Wood costs 100 Catnip; with Catnip Enrichment, the cost is lowered to **50 Catnip per unit of Wood**.

### **Strategic Utility**
In the very early stages of the game, wood is your primary building block for essential structures like Huts, Barns, and Libraries. Since your initial wood income relies heavily on manually refining surplus catnip before you have a large team of Woodcutter kittens, this upgrade effectively **doubles your early-game wood production velocity** from refinement. 

It is particularly useful during the Spring season when catnip production receives a +50% boost, allowing you to convert that massive seasonal surplus into building materials much more efficiently.
