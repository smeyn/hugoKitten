[Antimatter Fission]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Fission.md" >}}) is a late-game Workshop upgrade that enhances the industrial efficiency of your workforce, specifically targeting the production of [Eludium]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}).

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock this upgrade, you must first research the [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) science technology (which requires 500,000 Science and 1 Relic). Once the science is discovered, the upgrade becomes available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 525,000
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) 175
*   [Thorium:]( {{< relref "resource/thorium.md" >}}) 7,500

### **Core Effect**
The primary function of Antimatter Fission is to improve the productivity of [Engineers]( {{< relref "job/Engineer.md" >}})—the kittens that provide automated crafting:
*   **Effect:** It makes Engineers **25% more effective** when they are assigned to craft [Eludium]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}).

### **Strategic Utility**
Researching Antimatter Fission is a vital step for players entering the **Eludium Era** and the **Unicorn/Time Crystal Era** for several reasons:
*   **Unobtainium Conservation:** Eludium is extremely expensive, requiring **1,000 Unobtainium** per craft. Increasing the efficiency of your Engineers allows you to generate more of this rare resource for the same Unobtainium cost, which is critical since Unobtainium is a major late-game bottleneck.
*   **Scaling Infrastructure:** High yields of Eludium are necessary to afford [Eludium Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Eludium_Huts.md" >}}) (which significantly reduce the price ratio of housing) and advanced space structures like [Sunlifters]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) and **Cryostations**.
*   **Automated Management:** By improving Engineer effectiveness, players can rely more on passive, automated crafting to reach the high storage caps and material counts required for Phase 3 ("Endgame") progression.
