The [CAD System]( {{< relref "upgrade/CAD_System.md" >}}) is a mid-game **Workshop upgrade** that enhances your ability to craft blueprints by leveraging your existing scientific infrastructure. It effectively scales your crafting efficiency based on how many research buildings you have constructed.

### **Requirements and Prerequisites**
To unlock and purchase the CAD System, you must meet the following criteria:
*   **Science Cost:** 125,000.
*   **Titanium Cost:** 750.
*   **Technological Prerequisite:** You must have researched [Electronics]( {{< relref "techs/Electronics.md" >}}).

### **Mechanics and Effects**
The CAD System provides a **+1% bonus to your blueprint craft ratio** for every eligible scientific building in your village. This is particularly useful because blueprints are a major late-game bottleneck and normally require a high volume of science (25,000 per craft) to produce manually.

### **Eligible Buildings**
The bonus is calculated based on the total number of the following structures:
*   **Libraries** (or **Data Centres**).
*   **Academies**.
*   **Observatories**.
*   [Bio Labs]( {{< relref "buildings/Bio_Lab.md" >}}).

**Note:** Bio Labs contribute to this crafting bonus even if they are currently **turned off** or unpowered.

### **Strategic Utility**
In the mid-to-late game, your scientific infrastructure typically grows quite large to accommodate the escalating science caps required for advanced technologies. The CAD System turns this necessary expansion into a tool for **industrial scaling**, making the transition into the "Space Era"—which requires hundreds of blueprints for structures like Steamworks and Magnetos—significantly more efficient.
