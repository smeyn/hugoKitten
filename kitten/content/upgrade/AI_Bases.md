[AI Bases]( {{< relref "upgrade/AI_Bases.md" >}}) is a high-tier Workshop upgrade in *Kittens Game* that leverages your AI infrastructure to significantly expand space-based storage capacity.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To access this upgrade, you must have researched the [Artificial Intelligence]( {{< relref "techs/Artificial_Intelligence.md" >}}) science technology and established at least one [AI Core]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}). Once unlocked, it can be purchased in the Workshop for:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 750,000
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) 7,500

### **Core Effect: Moon Base Scaling**
The primary function of AI Bases is to create a multiplicative storage bonus based on your mega-structure population:
*   **Effect:** Every [AI Core]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}) you have built increases the effectiveness of your **Moon Bases** by **+10%**.
*   **Independence from AI Level:** Crucially, this bonus is derived strictly from the **number of AI Cores** owned. It is not affected by your current AI Level, Gigaflops count, or the threat of an AI Apocalypse.

### **Strategic Synergies**
*   **Transkittenism Policy:** If the [Transkittenism]( {{< relref "Policy/Transkittenism.md" >}}) policy is active (requiring a Leviathan trade), the bonus provided by AI Bases is further enhanced. Advanced guides indicate that under this policy, the bonus effectively increases to **11% per AI Core**.
*   **Unobtainium Capacity:** AI Bases are a critical component of the deep late-game "Chrono-Engine." Because Moon Bases are one of only two structures that provide **Unobtainium storage** (the other being Cryostations), scaling them with AI Cores is essential for reaching the **8.14 billion Unobtainium cap** required to build 100 Chronospheres for "positive resets".
*   **Antimatter Management:** Since the upgrade itself costs 7,500 Antimatter and requires AI Cores (which consume significant energy), players typically prioritize this after they have stabilized their [Sunlifter]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) production and **Containment Chamber** storage.

By combining a large number of AI Cores with the AI Bases upgrade, your Moon Bases—which initially provide only 150 Unobtainium storage each—can scale to support the massive resource requirements of the endgame.
