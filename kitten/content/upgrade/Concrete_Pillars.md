[Concrete Pillars]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}) is a **Workshop upgrade** that serves as the foundation for the "Concrete Era" of storage and housing. It is generally the first concrete-based upgrade players purchase after researching the [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) technology.

### **Costs and Requirements**
*   **Science Cost:** 100,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 50 Concrete.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research the [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) technology.

### **Core Effects**
Unlike the massive +75% boost from [Concrete Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}), this upgrade provides a smaller, universal reinforcement to your storage infrastructure:
*   **General Storage:** It increases the storage capacity of [Barns]( {{< relref "buildings/Barn.md" >}}), [Warehouses]( {{< relref "buildings/Warehouse.md" >}}), and **Harbors** by **+5%**.
*   **Gold Storage:** It increases the **Max Gold** capacity provided by [Mints]( {{< relref "buildings/Mint.md" >}}) by **+5%**.
*   **Catnip Synergy:** If you have the [Silos]( {{< relref "upgrade/Silos.md" >}}) upgrade, the +5% bonus also applies to the Catnip storage of your Barns and Warehouses.

### **Strategic Context**
While a 5% boost may seem small compared to other upgrades, Concrete Pillars is a critical stepping stone:
*   **Gateway Upgrade:** It is a direct prerequisite for unlocking [Concrete Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}) and [Concrete Warehouses]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Warehouses.md" >}}).
*   **Path to Concrete Huts:** It is part of the essential chain required to unlock [Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}), which is the primary goal for maximizing your population before your first reset.
