[Concrete Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}) is a **Workshop upgrade** that significantly increases the storage capacity of your Barns, marking a major step up in resource management during the mid-game "Concrete Era."

### **Core Effects**
*   **Storage Boost:** It increases the maximum storage of **Wood, Minerals, and Iron** provided by Barns by **+75%**.
*   **Catnip Synergy:** If you have researched the [Silos]( {{< relref "upgrade/Silos.md" >}}) upgrade, Concrete Barns also increases your **Catnip storage** (the bonus is 25% of the main effect, contributing to the total barn storage multiplier).

### **Costs and Requirements**
*   **Resources:** 45 Concrete and 2,000 Titanium.
*   **Science Cost:** 2,000 Science.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first purchase the [Concrete Pillars]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}) upgrade (which in turn requires the [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) technology).

### **Strategic Context**
*   **The Concrete Era:** This upgrade is part of a suite of "Concrete" improvements (including Concrete Warehouses and Concrete Huts) unlocked via [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) and [Concrete Pillars]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}). It is essential for breaking through the storage bottlenecks that typically occur after the "Titanium Era".
*   **Scaling:** Because it provides a percentage-based boost (+75%), its value scales with the number of Barns you have constructed. This increased capacity is often necessary to stockpile the massive amounts of Minerals and Wood required for late-game housing (Mansions) and space structures.
