[Dark Energy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Dark_Energy.md" >}}) is a specific **Workshop upgrade** in the late game that dramatically improves the utility of your [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}). It is part of the "Energy Rifts" upgrade chain, which transforms Accelerators from simple uranium producers into massive storage silos.

### **Core Effects**
*   **Storage Boost:** It increases the resource storage provided by [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}) by an additional **+250%**.
*   **Affected Resources:** This bonus applies to the storage caps for **Catnip, Wood, Minerals, Coal, Iron, Titanium, and Gold**.
*   **Cumulative Stacking:** This bonus is additive with other Accelerator upgrades. For example, if you have Stasis Chambers (+95%), Void Energy (+75%), and Dark Energy (+250%), your total storage bonus for Accelerators becomes +420%.

### **Requirements and Costs**
Dark Energy is an advanced upgrade that requires you to have entered the **Time Crystal** era.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have researched the [Void Energy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Energy.md" >}}) upgrade first.
*   **Science Cost:** 350,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 75 Eludium and **3 Time Crystals**.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Breaking Storage Bottlenecks:** By the time you reach the Eludium era, storage caps (particularly for Titanium and Unobtainium) become the primary hurdle for purchasing advanced buildings like [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}) and **Moon Bases**. Dark Energy provides a massive spike in capacity without requiring you to build new structures.
*   **Progression Chain:** It is a stepping stone to the final Accelerator upgrade, [Tachyon Accelerators]( {{< relref "upgrade/Tachyon_Accelerators.md" >}}) (which provides a further +500% storage), unlocked later via [Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}).
*   **Reset Preparation:** Increasing your storage caps is vital for **Chronosphere resets**. The more resources you can store, the more you can carry over to your next run.

*Note: Do not confuse "Dark Energy" with "Dark Future," which is a game state that triggers after year 40,000 and affects game mechanics like Time Impedance.*
