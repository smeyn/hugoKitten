[Navigation]( {{< relref "techs/Navigation.md" >}}) is a mid-game technology that acts as the essential bridge between terrestrial development and space exploration. It primarily enables the construction of [Trade Ships]( {{< relref "craft/Trade_Ship.md" >}}) and [Harbours]( {{< relref "buildings/Harbour.md" >}}), allowing your kittens to expand their storage limits and find new civilisations to trade for rare resources.

### **Technological Requirements**
To unlock Navigation, your civilisation must first research [Astronomy]( {{< relref "techs/Astronomy.md" >}}).
*   **Science Cost:** 35,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 100 Manuscripts.
*   **Unlocks:** Physics, Geology, and Architecture technologies.

### **The Starchart Economy**
The "fuel" for space navigation is the **Starchart**. These are rare resources used to build trade ships and launch space missions.

*   **Generation:** Starcharts are primarily found by observing **astronomical events** (0.25% base chance per day). Once you reach the space era, they are produced passively by [Satellites]( {{< relref "techs/Satellites.md" >}}) (0.005/sec), **Research Vessels** (0.05/sec), and **Space Beacons** (0.125/sec).
*   **Optimization:** Building **Observatories** is the most effective way to manage the starchart bottleneck. Each Observatory adds a 0.2% chance to see an event and a 1% chance for the event to succeed automatically without you clicking the "Observe" button.
*   **Kitten Jobs:** Researching the [Astrophysicists]( {{< relref "upgrade/Astrophysicists.md" >}}) upgrade allows your Scholar kittens to generate 0.0005 starcharts per second.

### **Space Missions and Logistics**
Once [Rocketry]( {{< relref "techs/Rocketry.md" >}}) is researched, the Space tab opens, allowing you to use your starcharts for navigation beyond Cath.

| Mission | Starchart Cost | Key Unlocks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [Orbital Launch]( {{< relref "space/Orbital_Launch.md" >}}) | 250 | Space Elevator, Moon Mission, Satellites |
| [Moon Mission]( {{< relref "space/Moon_Mission.md" >}}) | 500 | Lunar Outpost, Moon Base, Dune Mission |
| [Dune Mission]( {{< relref "space/Dune_Mission.md" >}}) | 1,000 | Planet Crackers, Hydraulic Fracturers |
| [Piscine Mission]( {{< relref "space/Piscine_Mission.md" >}}) | 1,500 | Research Vessels |

*Note: In the **Black Sky challenge**, the starchart requirement for the Orbital Launch is removed, but other resource costs increase by 11x.*

### **Key Navigation Upgrades**
*   [Satellite Navigation:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Satellite_Navigation.md" >}}) Each active satellite reduces the starchart cost of crafting Trade Ships by **1.25%**, capping at a **75% discount** once you have 45 satellites.
*   **Antimatter & Thorium Drives:** These upgrades significantly reduce mission travel times. An Antimatter Drive divides travel time by 25, a Thorium Drive by 50, and both together by **75**.
*   [Caravanserai:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Caravanserai.md" >}}) This Workshop upgrade allows your [Tradeposts]( {{< relref "buildings/Tradepost.md" >}}) to improve your standing with hostile races like the Zebras, making trade navigation safer.
