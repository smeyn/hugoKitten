[Astrophysicists]( {{< relref "upgrade/Astrophysicists.md" >}}) is a late-mid-game Workshop upgrade that allows your [Scholar]( {{< relref "job/Scholar.md" >}}) kittens to produce **starcharts** passively. This is a critical milestone for transitioning from manual observation of astronomical events to automated starchart production for deep-space exploration.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock and purchase this upgrade, you must meet the following requirements:
*   **Technology:** You must first research the [Orbital Engineering]( {{< relref "techs/Orbital_Engineering.md" >}}) science technology.
*   **Science Cost:** 250,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 350 [Unobtainium]( {{< relref "resource/unobtainium.md" >}}).

### **Core Effect**
The primary function of the Astrophysicists upgrade is to augment the Scholar job:
*   **Starchart Generation:** Every kitten assigned to the Scholar job will generate **+0.0005 starcharts per second**.
*   **Continued Science Output:** Scholars continue to produce their base science (+0.035 per tick) while simultaneously generating starcharts.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Automating Space Missions:** Early in the game, starcharts are primarily obtained by manually clicking on astronomical events in the log. Astrophysicists provides a reliable, passive income of starcharts, which are mandatory for launching rockets and establishing space missions to planets like Redmoon, Dune, and Piscine.
*   **Scaling Starchart Production:** While individual Scholars provide a small amount, a large population of Scholars (boosted by upgrades like **Neural Network**) can significantly accelerate starchart accumulation.
*   **Complementary Structures:** This upgrade works alongside other starchart-generating structures such as [Satellites]( {{< relref "techs/Satellites.md" >}}) (+0.005/sec), **Research Vessels** (+0.05/sec), and **Space Beacons** (+0.125/sec) to meet the high starchart demands of late-game missions.
