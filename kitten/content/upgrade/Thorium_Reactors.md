[Thorium Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Thorium_Reactors.md" >}}) is a high-tier late-game Workshop upgrade that enhances the power output of your standard [Reactor]( {{< relref "buildings/Reactor.md" >}}) buildings. It represents a shift towards more advanced nuclear fuels to meet the escalating energy demands of space exploration and artificial intelligence.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Science Cost:** 400,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 10,000 Thorium.
*   **Prerequisite Technology:** You must first research the [Thorium]( {{< relref "resource/thorium.md" >}}) science technology, which is unlocked after Orbital Engineering.

### **Core Effects and Mechanics**
The upgrade allows your existing Reactors to utilise Thorium as a secondary, more potent fuel source:
*   **Energy Boost:** It provides a **+25% increase** to the energy production of every Reactor. Given that a base Reactor produces 10 Watts, this upgrade typically adds **+2.5 Watts** per building.
*   **Fuel Consumption:** This boost comes at the cost of **Thorium consumption**, which is depleted at a rate of 0.05 to 0.25 units per tick depending on your current level of automation and research.
*   **Toggleable Functionality:** Like many advanced industrial buildings, the Thorium consumption (and its associated energy bonus) can be toggled on or off to manage resource levels.

### **Strategic Importance**
*   **Supporting the Endgame Grid:** Thorium Reactors are essential for powering energy-heavy structures such as [AI Cores]( {{< relref "buildings/AI_Core.md" >}}), **Entanglement Stations**, and **Terraforming Stations**.
*   **Antimatter Generation:** A common strategy is to only activate Thorium Reactors during **time skips** to ensure a positive energy state. This is vital because [Sunlifters]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) only produce antimatter if your energy production is positive when the year changes.
*   **Efficiency Upgrades:** Once Thorium Reactors are active, you should prioritise the [Enriched Thorium]( {{< relref "upgrade/Enriched_Thorium.md" >}}) upgrade. This reduces the fuel consumption of your reactors by **25%**, allowing your Thorium stockpiles to last significantly longer.

In specialised challenge runs like **Iron Will**, where kittens are unavailable, the energy boost from Thorium Reactors is often a mandatory requirement for reaching the high power thresholds needed for deep-space missions.
