[Alloy Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Barns.md" >}}) is a major late-mid-game Workshop upgrade that significantly expands your village's storage capacity for basic resources. It is considered a key milestone for transitioning out of the early game and preparing for space exploration.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock Alloy Barns, you must first research the [Chemistry]( {{< relref "techs/Chemistry.md" >}}) science technology (which requires Physics and Navigation). Once researched, the upgrade is available in the Workshop for:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 75,000
*   [Alloy:]( {{< relref "craft/Alloy.md" >}}) 20
*   [Plate:]( {{< relref "resource/plate.md" >}}) 750

### **Core Effects**
The primary function of Alloy Barns is to double the base storage effectiveness of your primary storage buildings:
*   **+100% Storage Bonus:** It increases the maximum amount of **wood, minerals, and iron** that can be stored in **Barns, Warehouses, and Harbours** by 100%.
*   **Catnip Storage Synergy:** If you have already purchased the [Silos]( {{< relref "upgrade/Silos.md" >}}) workshop upgrade, Alloy Barns will also apply **25% of its effect to your maximum catnip storage**.

### **Strategic Importance**
*   **Affording Expensive Upgrades:** As you progress toward Phase 2 (Space), the science and resource costs for technologies like [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) and [Electronics]( {{< relref "techs/Electronics.md" >}}) escalate rapidly. Alloy Barns is often necessary to raise your resource caps high enough to purchase these advanced techs.
*   **Kitten Population Growth:** Expanding storage is a prerequisite for building more housing, such as [Mansions]( {{< relref "buildings/Mansion.md" >}}) or additional [Log Houses]( {{< relref "buildings/Log_House.md" >}}), which in turn allows for a larger kitten population and more industrial output.
*   **Titanium Management:** Strategic guides note that Alloy-tier buildings and upgrades require substantial amounts of [Titanium]( {{< relref "resource/titanium.md" >}}). Because Titanium is primarily gained through trading with Zebras, you will likely need to build a significant number of [Trade Ships]( {{< relref "craft/Trade_Ship.md" >}}) and **Observatories** to fund the Alloy production needed for this upgrade.
*   **Progression Milestone:** Players typically aim to acquire all Alloy-based storage upgrades (including [Alloy Warehouses]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Warehouses.md" >}})) when they reach approximately **90–100 kittens**. This provides the storage foundation required to reach the **Concrete era** and eventually produce [Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) for a more efficient reset.
