**Advanced Antimatter Reactors** (Advanced AM Reactors) is a high-tier Workshop upgrade that significantly expands your civilization's maximum science capacity by enhancing space-based research structures.

### **Acquisition and Costs**
*   **Prerequisite Upgrade:** You must first purchase [Antimatter Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Reactors.md" >}}).
*   **Resource Costs:** **70 Eludium** and **1,750 Antimatter**.

### **Core Effects**
The primary function of this upgrade is to boost the effectiveness of two specific space structures:
*   **Science Cap Increase:** It raises the maximum science bonus provided by **Research Vessels** and **Space Beacons** to **150%**.
*   **Relative Efficiency:** This represents approximately a **77% increase in effectiveness** over the standard Antimatter Reactors upgrade.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Breaking Science Bottlenecks:** This upgrade is a critical component of the late-game "Antimatter Phase". It is often necessary to reach the massive science caps required for endgame technologies, such as [Quantum Cryptography]( {{< relref "techs/Quantum_Cryptography.md" >}}) (1.25M Science).
*   **Endgame Progression:** By maximizing the science storage of Space Beacons, players can better support [Relic Station]( {{< relref "upgrade/Relic_Station.md" >}}) operations and general research velocity.
*   **Upgrade Path:** Advanced AM Reactors is the penultimate tier of science-boosting reactor upgrades, eventually being succeeded by [Void Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Reactors.md" >}}), which increase the science bonus from these structures to **400%**.
