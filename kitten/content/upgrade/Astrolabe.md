The [Astrolabe]( {{< relref "upgrade/Astrolabe.md" >}}) is a crucial Workshop upgrade in *Kittens Game* that enhances the effectiveness of the [Observatory]( {{< relref "buildings/Observatory.md" >}}) building, primarily by boosting your capacity for science storage.

### Details of Astrolabe

| Category | Specification | Citation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Type** | Workshop Upgrade for the Observatory building |, |
| **Prerequisite** | Unlocked after researching the [Navigation]( {{< relref "techs/Navigation.md" >}}) technology |, |
| **Cost** | 25,000 Science, 5 Titanium, and 75 Starchart | |
| **Effect** | Improves Observatory effectiveness by **50%**, specifically increasing the Observatory's **Max Science** storage by **+50%** |, |

The standard Observatory building already increases maximum Science storage by 1,000. With the Astrolabe upgrade, this capacity is further boosted by 50%.
