[Cryocomputing]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cryocomputing.md" >}}) is a late-game **Workshop upgrade** that significantly improves the energy efficiency of your scientific infrastructure, specifically targeting [Data Centers]( {{< relref "buildings/Data_Center.md" >}}).

### **Core Effects**
*   **Energy Reduction:** It reduces the energy consumption of [Data Centers]( {{< relref "buildings/Data_Center.md" >}}) by **50%**.
*   **Specific Value:** This drops the power usage of each Data Center from **2 Watts to 1 Watt**.

### **Requirements and Costs**
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research the [Superconductors]( {{< relref "techs/Superconductors.md" >}}) technology.
*   **Science Cost:** 125,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 15 Eludium.

### **Strategic Utility**
Cryocomputing becomes essential when you transition from **Libraries** to [Data Centers]( {{< relref "buildings/Data_Center.md" >}}) to boost your science cap.

*   **The Energy Bottleneck:** While Data Centers provide vastly superior stats to Libraries (offering +750 Max Science and +250 Max Culture), they come with a steep upkeep cost of **2 Watts** per building. Since you typically need hundreds of Data Centers to reach the science caps required for endgame technologies (like [Void Space]( {{< relref "techs/Void_Space.md" >}}) or [Paradox Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Paradox_Theory.md" >}})), this can drain hundreds of Watts from your grid.
*   **Efficiency:** By halving this consumption, Cryocomputing essentially refunds massive amounts of power, allowing you to reallocate that energy to other heavy consumers like **Containment Chambers**, [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}), or **Space Stations** without needing to build as many Sunlifters.
