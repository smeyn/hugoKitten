[Stasis Chambers]( {{< relref "upgrade/Stasis_Chambers.md" >}}) are a high-tier Workshop upgrade that significantly enhances the storage capacity of your [Accelerators]( {{< relref "buildings/Accelerator.md" >}}). It is a key component in scaling resource caps during the late game, particularly for deep runs where standard storage buildings reach their limits.

### **Technological Unlock and Cost**
*   **Prerequisite:** You must first research [Chronophysics]( {{< relref "techs/Chronophysics.md" >}}).
*   **Science Cost:** 235,000.
*   **Resource Cost:** 200 Alloy, 2,000 Uranium, and **1 Time Crystal**.

### **Core Effect: Energy Rift Enhancement**
The primary function of Stasis Chambers is to augment the [Energy Rifts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Energy_Rifts.md" >}}) upgrade, which allows Accelerators to store basic materials in pocket dimensions.
*   **Storage Bonus:** Each Stasis Chamber increases the maximum storage for **catnip, wood, minerals, coal, iron, titanium, and gold** provided by Accelerators by **+95%**.
*   **Additive Nature:** This bonus stacks additively with other Accelerator storage upgrades, such as [Void Energy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Energy.md" >}}) (+75%), [Dark Energy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Dark_Energy.md" >}}) (+250%), and [Tachyon Accelerators]( {{< relref "upgrade/Tachyon_Accelerators.md" >}}) (+500%).

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Deep Runs:** Because this upgrade costs a **Time Crystal**, it is generally recommended only when you are performing a **"Deep Run"**. Using rare Time Crystals on Workshop upgrades that do not carry over across resets can be considered a waste in shorter runs unless you can easily replenish them.
*   **Storage Bottlenecks:** This upgrade is vital for bypassing major mid-to-late game bottlenecks, such as the titanium and gold caps required to afford advanced space structures or expensive religious upgrades.
*   **Non-Permanence:** Unlike metaphysics upgrades, **Stasis Chambers are lost upon resetting** the game. You will need to repurchase the upgrade in every subsequent run to regain its storage benefits.

By effectively doubling the effectiveness of Energy Rifts, Stasis Chambers represent the first major step in transforming your industrial base from production-only into a critical component of your global storage network.
