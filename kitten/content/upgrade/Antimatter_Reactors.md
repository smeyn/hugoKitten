[Antimatter Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Reactors.md" >}}) is a late-game Workshop upgrade that significantly expands your science storage capacity by enhancing your space-based research infrastructure.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
*   **Technology Unlock:** You must first research the [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) science technology.
*   **Resource Cost:** The upgrade requires **750 Antimatter** and **35 Eludium**.

### **Core Effects**
The primary function of this upgrade is to boost the effectiveness of your primary endgame science structures:
*   **Science Storage Boost:** It increases the maximum science storage provided by **Research Vessels** and **Space Beacons** by **95%**.
*   **Efficiency:** The upgrade is described as making these structures "95% more effective" overall.

### **Endgame Progression**
Antimatter Reactors is the first in a chain of three upgrades focused on scaling your science cap through space structures:
1.  [Antimatter Reactors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Reactors.md" >}}) +95% Max Science.
2.  [Advanced AM Reactors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Advanced_AM_Reactors.md" >}}) Increases the total science bonus to **150%** (requires 70 Eludium and 1,750 Antimatter).
3.  [Void Reactors:]( {{< relref "upgrade/Void_Reactors.md" >}}) Increases the total science bonus by **400%** (requires 250 Void and 2,500 Antimatter).

This progression is vital for reaching the high science caps necessary for "Endgame" (Phase 3) technologies like [Quantum Cryptography]( {{< relref "techs/Quantum_Cryptography.md" >}}) (1.25M Science) or [Blackchain]( {{< relref "techs/Blackchain.md" >}}) (5M Science). While early-game science relies on Libraries and Academies, the bonuses from Antimatter Reactors allow Research Vessels (base +10k science) and Space Beacons (base +25k science) to become your dominant sources of science capacity.
