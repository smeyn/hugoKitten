[Alloy Armour]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Armour.md" >}}) is a mid-to-late game **Workshop upgrade** that further improves the efficiency of your [Hunters]( {{< relref "job/Hunter.md" >}}), serving as the fourth tier of weapon technology.

### **Core Effects**
*   **Hunting Bonus:** It provides a **+0.5 increase** to your civilization's hunting bonus.
*   **Yield Increase:** This bonus translates to roughly a **40–50% increase** in the base amount of [Furs]( {{< relref "resource/Fur.md" >}}) and [Ivory]( {{< relref "resource/ivory.md" >}}) obtained per hunt.
*   **Additive Stacking:** Like previous armor upgrades, this bonus is additive. For example, if you already have Bolas (+1), Hunting Armour (+2), and Steel Armour (+0.5), adding Alloy Armour brings your total hunting bonus to **4.0**.

### **Requirements and Costs**
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research the [Chemistry]( {{< relref "techs/Chemistry.md" >}}) technology to unlock this upgrade.
*   **Science Cost:** 50,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 25 Alloy.

### **Strategic Context**
*   **Diminishing Returns:** Compared to [Hunting Armour]( {{< relref "upgrade/Hunting_Armour.md" >}}) (which gives +2.0) or [Bolas]( {{< relref "upgrade/Bolas.md" >}}) (which gives +1.0), the +0.5 bonus from Alloy Armour is relatively small. However, by this stage of the game, increasing your [Ivory]( {{< relref "resource/ivory.md" >}}) income is crucial for trading with the [Nagas]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Nagas.md" >}}) (to get Minerals) and for the **Unicorn religion** (Minting ivory/sacrificing).
*   **Progression:** It acts as a stepping stone toward the final hunter upgrade, [Nanosuits]( {{< relref "upgrade/Nanosuits.md" >}}) (unlocked via Nanotechnology), which provides another +0.5 bonus.
