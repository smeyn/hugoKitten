[Bolas]( {{< relref "upgrade/Bolas.md" >}}) is an early-game Workshop upgrade that serves as a throwing weapon to improve the results of your hunts. You can unlock this upgrade after researching the [Mining]( {{< relref "techs/Mining.md" >}}) science technology. It costs **1,000 Science, 250 Minerals, and 50 Wood** to purchase.

The primary effect of Bolas is to provide a flat **+1.0 hunting bonus**. This bonus makes your hunters **twice as effective**, resulting in an 80% to 100% increase in the amount of fur and ivory collected per hunt. Specifically, the expected fur yield increases from 39.50 to **71.50**, and the expected ivory yield increases from 10.78 to **20.24**.

These bonuses are **additive** with other hunting upgrades. For example, if you have researched both Bolas (+1.0) and Hunting Armour (+2.0), your total hunting bonus will be 3.0. This increased bonus expands the range of random integers the game uses to calculate your total resources per hunt.

In **Iron Will** mode, Bolas also helps with gold acquisition. Once [Gold Ore]( {{< relref "upgrade/Gold_Ore.md" >}}) is researched in this mode, each hunt has a 25% chance of yielding gold, and the amount produced is partially determined by your current hunting bonus
.
