[Cold Fusion]( {{< relref "upgrade/Cold_Fusion.md" >}}) is a Workshop upgrade that significantly improves the power output of your [Reactors]( {{< relref "buildings/Reactor.md" >}}). It is a critical mid-to-late game upgrade for managing the increasing energy demands of space structures and advanced automation.

### **Requirements and Costs**
To unlock Cold Fusion, you must first research the [Superconductors]( {{< relref "techs/Superconductors.md" >}}) technology.
*   **Science Cost:** 200,000
*   **Eludium Cost:** 25
*   **Prerequisite Tech:** Superconductors

### **Core Effects**
*   **Energy Boost:** It increases the energy output of all Reactors by **25%**.
*   **Production Value:** Specifically, it raises the base energy production of a Reactor by **2.5 Watts** (typically increasing it from 10 to 12.5 Watts).

### **Strategic Utility**
Cold Fusion acts as a major "step up" for your energy infrastructure during the **Eludium Era**. 
*   **Powering Space:** As you build energy-heavy structures like **Containment Chambers** (50 Watts) and **Entanglement Stations** (25 Watts), the passive boost from Cold Fusion helps maintain a positive energy balance without needing to build excessive numbers of Sunlifters immediately.
*   **Efficiency:** Unlike [Thorium Reactors]( {{< relref "upgrade/Thorium_Reactors.md" >}}), which require a constant fuel source (Thorium) to provide their bonus, Cold Fusion provides a permanent, passive increase to reactor output with no ongoing resource cost.
