[Antimatter Bases]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Bases.md" >}}) is a late-game Workshop upgrade that significantly improves the energy efficiency of your space infrastructure by optimizing **Moon Bases**.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock this upgrade, you must first research the [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) science technology. Once researched, the upgrade is available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) 250
*   [Eludium:]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}) 15

### **Core Effect**
The primary function of Antimatter Bases is to reduce the high energy cost of maintaining bases on Redmoon:
*   **50% Energy Reduction:** It halves the energy consumption of every **Moon Base**.
*   **Impact:** A standard Moon Base consumes 10 Watts (Wt) of energy; with this upgrade, the drain is reduced to **5 Wt** per building.

### **Strategic Importance**
*   **Energy Management:** As you progress into the late game, energy becomes a major bottleneck for advanced structures like Containment Chambers and AI Cores. Reducing the drain from your primary storage buildings (Moon Bases) is critical for maintaining a **positive energy balance**.
*   **Antimatter Production Synergy:** Maintaining a positive energy surplus is the mandatory requirement for [Sunlifters]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) to generate antimatter at the change of each year. By lowering Moon Base power consumption, Antimatter Bases helps ensure your civilization continues to produce the very resource needed for further endgame expansion.
*   **Scaling Storage:** Moon Bases provide essential storage for Unobtainium, Titanium, and Oil. Reducing their power cost allows you to build more bases, which in turn increases your resource caps for even more advanced space missions and technologies.
