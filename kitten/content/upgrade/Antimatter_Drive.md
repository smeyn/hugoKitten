The [Antimatter Drive]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Drive.md" >}}) is a high-tier late-game Workshop upgrade that functions as an advanced rocket engine to significantly increase the speed of space exploration.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To access this upgrade, you must first research the [Antimatter]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) science technology, which requires 500,000 Science and 1 Relic. Once unlocked, the Antimatter Drive can be purchased in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 450,000
*   [Antimatter:]( {{< relref "resource/Antimatter.md" >}}) 125

### **Core Effects on Space Travel**
The primary function of the Antimatter Drive is to drastically reduce the real-time duration of space missions:
*   **Travel Time Reduction:** Researching this upgrade **divides all listed space travel times by 25**.
*   **Thorium Drive Synergy:** If the [Thorium Drive]( {{< relref "upgrade/Thorium_Drive.md" >}}) has also been researched, their combined efficiency **divides all space travel times by 75**.

### **Strategic Importance**
This upgrade is essential for reaching the most distant reaches of the Helios system and beyond, where base travel times are otherwise prohibitive. For instance, without any drive upgrades, a mission to [Umbra]( {{< relref "planet/Umbra.md" >}}) takes over 4 hours of real-time, and a mission to **Centaurus** takes over 66 hours. By applying the Antimatter Drive (especially in tandem with the Thorium Drive), these times are reduced to manageable levels, allowing you to establish endgame infrastructure like **HR Harvesters** and **Tectonic** stations much faster.

Additionally, while these drives provide a permanent passive speed increase, players can further reduce travel times for missions already in progress by **combusting Time Crystals** in the Time tab.
