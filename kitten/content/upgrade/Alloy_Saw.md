The [Alloy Saw]( {{< relref "upgrade/Alloy_Saw.md" >}}) is a late-mid-game Workshop upgrade that enhances the passive wood production of your [Lumber Mills]( {{< relref "buildings/Lumber_Mill.md" >}}).

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock the Alloy Saw, you must first research the [Chemistry]( {{< relref "techs/Chemistry.md" >}}) science technology. Once unlocked, it is available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 85,000
*   [Alloy:]( {{< relref "craft/Alloy.md" >}}) 75
*   **Prerequisite Upgrade:** You must have already purchased the [Titanium Saw]( {{< relref "upgrade/Titanium_Saw.md" >}}).

### **Core Effect**
The primary function of this upgrade is to boost the efficiency of every Lumber Mill in your village:
*   **Production Bonus:** It increases the wood production bonus of each Lumber Mill by an additional **15%**.
*   **Scaling:** This bonus is additive with previous saw upgrades (Reinforced, Steel, and Titanium Saws). A base Lumber Mill provides +10% wood production; with the full chain of upgrades including the Alloy Saw, each mill becomes significantly more productive.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Late-Game Wood Velocity:** As the costs for advanced structures like **Observatories** and [Harbours]( {{< relref "buildings/Harbour.md" >}}) escalate, maintaining a high wood production rate is critical. The Alloy Saw allows you to continue scaling wood income without needing to constantly build more mills, which eventually become prohibitively expensive.
*   **Efficiency Benchmark:** Mathematically, moving from 40 to 50 Lumber Mills with an Alloy Saw provides approximately a **21.4% relative increase** in wood production, compared to only a 20% increase if no saws were researched.
*   **Crafting Chain:** Because wood is a foundational resource for second-order crafts like **Beams** and [Scaffolds]( {{< relref "craft/Scaffold.md" >}}), this upgrade helps speed up the production of materials required for early space exploration and the transition into the **Titanium era**.
