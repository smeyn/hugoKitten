[Thorium Drive]( {{< relref "upgrade/Thorium_Drive.md" >}}) is a late-game Workshop upgrade that functions as a high-performance rocket engine, significantly accelerating the speed of space exploration.

### **Unlock and Construction Costs**
*   **Prerequisite Technology:** You must first research the **Thorium science technology** (375,000 Science and 375 Blueprints), which becomes available after [Orbital Engineering]( {{< relref "techs/Orbital_Engineering.md" >}}).
*   **Upgrade Costs:** To purchase the Thorium Drive in the Workshop, you need:
    *   **400,000 Science**.
    *   **10,000 Ships**.
    *   **40,000 Gears**.
    *   **2,000 Alloy**.
    *   **100,000 Thorium**.

### **Core Effects on Space Travel**
The primary function of the Thorium Drive is to drastically reduce the duration of space missions by increasing travel speed.
*   **Travel Time Reduction:** Researching this upgrade **divides all listed space travel times by 50**. 
*   **Antimatter Drive Synergy:** If the [Antimatter Drive]( {{< relref "upgrade/Antimatter_Drive.md" >}}) (unlocked via the Antimatter technology) is also researched, the combined effect **divides travel times by 75**.
*   **Strategic Utility:** This speed boost is essential for reaching the most distant points of the Helios system and beyond, where base travel times are otherwise prohibitive. For example, without upgrades, the mission to **Centaurus** takes over **66 hours** of real-time, and [Umbra]( {{< relref "planet/Umbra.md" >}}) takes over **4 hours**.

### **Additional Speed Modifiers**
While the Thorium Drive provides a permanent passive speed increase, players can also **combust Time Crystals** to instantly reduce the travel time of missions currently in progress. The drive upgrades are particularly vital in the late game to reduce the "dead time" between launching missions and being able to construct new space-based infrastructure.
