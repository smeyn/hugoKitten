[Augmentations]( {{< relref "upgrade/Augmentations.md" >}}) is a late-game Workshop upgrade that significantly boosts the productivity of your entire workforce by enhancing the effectiveness of kitten job skills.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock this upgrade, you must first research the [Nanotechnology]( {{< relref "techs/Nanotechnology.md" >}}) science technology. Once researched, the upgrade becomes available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 150,000
*   [Titanium:]( {{< relref "resource/titanium.md" >}}) 5,000
*   [Uranium:]( {{< relref "resource/uranium.md" >}}) 50

### **Core Effect: Skill Enhancement**
The primary function of Augmentations is to make kitten job skills **100% more effective**.
*   **Broad Impact:** Because it targets the skill system itself, this upgrade provides a productivity increase for **all jobs**, including Woodcutters, Farmers, Scholars, Hunters, Miners, Priests, and Geologists.
*   **Mechanical Scaling:** The bonus from Augmentations **multiplies** with the base skill bonus a kitten has earned through experience (such as the +18.75% bonus from a Master-level kitten).
*   **Additive Stacking:** This 100% boost is **additive** with the [Logistics]( {{< relref "upgrade/Logistics.md" >}}) workshop upgrade, which provides a separate 15% increase to skill effectiveness. When both are researched, your kittens' total skill-based production bonuses are increased by 115%.

### **Strategic Utility**
Augmentations is a vital milestone for late-game industrial scaling. As your kittens reach higher mastery levels (like **Master** or **Proficient**), the multiplicative nature of this upgrade allows you to reach significantly higher production yields for the same population. This is especially useful for overcoming mid-to-late game bottlenecks in wood, minerals, and science, as it improves the output of every specialized worker simultaneously.
