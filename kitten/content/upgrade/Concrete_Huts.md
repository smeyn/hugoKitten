[Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) is a pivotal **Workshop upgrade** that marks the end of the "early game" and is considered the primary goal for your first run. It drastically reduces the cost scaling of [Huts]( {{< relref "buildings/Hut.md" >}}), allowing for a massive population boom.

### **Core Effects and Costs**
*   **Effect:** Reduces the price ratio of Huts by **30%**.
    *   Huts have a very steep base price ratio (2.5). This upgrade, combined with the earlier [Ironwood Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Ironwood_Huts.md" >}}) (-50%), makes high-level Huts significantly affordable.
*   **Cost:** 125,000 Science, 45 Concrete, and 3,000 Titanium.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) (which unlocks Concrete Pillars) and typically need to have purchased [Concrete Pillars]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}) first.

### **Strategic Importance: The First Reset**
Concrete Huts is the single most important milestone for determining when to perform your **first reset**.
*   **The 130 Kitten Goal:** Without this upgrade, most players cap out around 70–80 kittens. Concrete Huts allows you to push your population to **130–140 kittens**.
*   **Paragon Farming:** Since you earn 1 Paragon for every kitten over 70, reaching 130 kittens guarantees you **60 Paragon** upon resetting. This is the recommended amount needed to buy essential Metaphysics upgrades (like [Diplomacy]( {{< relref "Policy/Diplomacy.md" >}}) and [Golden Ratio]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Golden_Ratio.md" >}})) for your second run.

### **Requirements and The "Concrete Era"**
Reaching Concrete Huts requires overcoming two main bottlenecks:
1.  **Science Cap:** The 125,000 Science cost is often higher than your caps allow. You will likely need to build [Bio Labs]( {{< relref "buildings/Bio_Lab.md" >}}) (unlocked by Biology) or craft extensive [Compendiums]( {{< relref "resource/Compendium.md" >}}) to raise your maximum science limit enough to afford it.
2.  [Titanium:]( {{< relref "resource/titanium.md" >}}) The 3,000 Titanium cost requires a robust trade network with the [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}). You should rely on [Trade Ships]( {{< relref "craft/Trade_Ship.md" >}}) and [Geodesy]( {{< relref "upgrade/Geodesy.md" >}}) (if unlocked) to stockpile this Titanium.

Once you purchase Concrete Huts, the strategy is simple: build as many Huts as your storage allows, maximize your population, and then execute the **BMF** (Backup, Metas, Faith) checklist before resetting.
