[Telecommunication]( {{< relref "upgrade/Telecommunication.md" >}}) is a late-mid-game Workshop upgrade that drastically accelerates the rate at which your workforce gains professional experience. It represents a shift towards a more connected society where kittens can share knowledge and master their trades more efficiently.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To unlock this upgrade, you must first research the [Electronics]( {{< relref "techs/Electronics.md" >}}) science technology. Once researched, the upgrade becomes available in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 150,000
*   [Titanium:]( {{< relref "resource/titanium.md" >}}) 5,000
*   [Uranium:]( {{< relref "resource/uranium.md" >}}) 50

### **Core Effect: Rapid Skill Acquisition**
The primary function of Telecommunication is to modify the speed at which kittens gain job skills. 
*   **Mechanism:** It sets the **base learning rate percentage for job skills equal to your current number of kittens**, provided you have a population of **at least 100 kittens**.
*   **Impact:** By scaling learning speed directly with population, your kittens will reach high proficiency levels—such as **Proficient (+13%)** or **Master (+18.75%)**—significantly faster than through standard manual work alone.

### **Strategic Utility**
Telecommunication is highly valuable for maintaining high production efficiency across multiple runs. Because job skills provide **multiplicative productivity bonuses** that stack with happiness and other global multipliers, having a fully "Mastered" workforce is essential for maximizing resource yields. This upgrade is particularly useful in the late game when you are frequently assigning new kittens to specialized roles or moving them between professions to address specific resource bottlenecks.
