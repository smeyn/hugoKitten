The term [Planet Busters]( {{< relref "upgrade/Planet_Busters.md" >}}) refers to a highly effective late-game **Workshop upgrade** in *Kittens Game* that dramatically increases uranium production.

### Upgrade Details

| Category | Specification | Citation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Target Structure** | **Planet Cracker** (a vessel known as the USS Mining Vessel Hissmeowra) |, |
| **Prerequisite Technology** | **Exometallurgy** (previously known as Advanced Exogeology), |,, |
| **Effect** | The upgrade makes the Planet Cracker's output **twice as effective**, providing a bonus of **+100\% Uranium Production**. |, |

The Planet Cracker structure itself is unlocked after successfully completing the Dune Mission, and its base function is to produce Uranium,. The Planet Busters upgrade doubles the efficiency of this production.,
