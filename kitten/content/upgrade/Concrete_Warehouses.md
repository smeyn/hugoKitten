[Concrete Warehouses]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Warehouses.md" >}}) is a mid-game **Workshop upgrade** that further expands your storage infrastructure during the "Concrete Era." It serves as a vital companion to [Concrete Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}), helping you reach the resource caps necessary for advanced housing and space technologies.

### **Core Effects**
*   **Storage Boost:** It increases the storage capacity of [Warehouses]( {{< relref "buildings/Warehouse.md" >}}), [Barns]( {{< relref "buildings/Barn.md" >}}), **Harbors**, and [Mints]( {{< relref "buildings/Mint.md" >}}) by **+35%**.
*   **Resources Affects:** This multiplier applies to your maximum **Wood, Minerals, Coal, Iron, Gold, and Titanium**.
*   **Catnip Synergy:** If you have the [Silos]( {{< relref "upgrade/Silos.md" >}}) upgrade, Concrete Warehouses also increases the **Catnip** capacity of your storage buildings (contributing 25% of its total effect, or roughly +8.75%).

### **Costs and Requirements**
*   **Science Cost:** 100,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 35 Concrete and 1,250 Titanium.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must have researched [Mechanization]( {{< relref "techs/Mechanization.md" >}}) and purchased the [Concrete Pillars]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Pillars.md" >}}) upgrade first.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **The Concrete Suite:** This upgrade is part of the critical trio of concrete upgrades (Pillars, Barns, Warehouses). While [Concrete Barns]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Barns.md" >}}) offers a higher percentage boost (+75%), Concrete Warehouses is essential because it scales your **Harbors**, which become your primary source of storage in the mid-to-late game.
*   **Pre-Reset Push:** Players often purchase this alongside [Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) to maximize their storage limits, allowing them to build a few extra [Log Houses]( {{< relref "buildings/Log_House.md" >}}) or [Mansions]( {{< relref "buildings/Mansion.md" >}}) to squeeze out more kittens (and thus more Paragon) before resetting.
*   **Gold and Titanium:** Unlike early barn upgrades, Concrete Warehouses boosts **Gold and Titanium** storage (via Harbors and Warehouses), which is critical for affording the **125,000 Science** cap required for technologies like [Electronics]( {{< relref "techs/Electronics.md" >}}) and [Robotics]( {{< relref "techs/Robotics.md" >}}).
