The [Crossbow]( {{< relref "upgrade/Crossbow.md" >}}) is a mid-game **Workshop upgrade** that further improves the efficiency of your hunting workforce. It serves as the second tier of weapon technology, following the [Composite Bow]( {{< relref "upgrade/Composite_Bow.md" >}}).

### **Core Effects**
*   **Production Boost:** It provides a permanent **+25% bonus** to **Catpower production** for all kittens assigned to the [Hunter]( {{< relref "job/Hunter.md" >}}) job.
*   **Cumulative Effect:** This bonus stacks additively with other weapon upgrades (like the Composite Bow's +50%), contributing to your total hunting multiplier.

### **Costs and Requirements**
*   **Science Cost:** 12,000 Science.
*   **Resource Cost:** 1,500 Iron.
*   **Prerequisite:** You must research the [Machinery]( {{< relref "techs/Machinery.md" >}}) technology to unlock this upgrade.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Hunting Frequency:** Like the Composite Bow, the primary value of the Crossbow is increasing your **Catpower** cap fill rate. This allows you to send hunts more frequently to gather [Furs]( {{< relref "resource/Fur.md" >}}) (for Parchment/Manuscripts) and [Ivory]( {{< relref "resource/ivory.md" >}}) (for happiness and trade).
*   **Iron Will Synergy:** Since you are interested in **Iron Will** mode: The Crossbow upgrade applies to [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}), who act as your Hunters in the absence of kittens. Purchasing this will boost your Zebras' passive Catpower generation by 25%, which is vital for maintaining trade with races like the [Nagas]( {{< relref "Civilisations/Nagas.md" >}}) and [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}).
*   **Pacifism Challenge:** Note that if you ever attempt the **Pacifism** challenge, weapon upgrades like the Crossbow suffer a penalty, becoming **-10% effective** per completion.
