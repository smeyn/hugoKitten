[Chronoengineers]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoengineers.md" >}}) is a high-tier late-game Workshop upgrade that enhances the capabilities of your Engineer kittens, specifically allowing them to function during temporal manipulations.

### **Costs and Prerequisites**
To access this upgrade, you must first research the [Tachyon Theory]( {{< relref "techs/Tachyon_Theory.md" >}}) science technology. Once unlocked, it can be purchased in the Workshop for the following resources:
*   [Science:]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) 500,000
*   [Time Crystals:]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) 5
*   [Eludium:]( {{< relref "resource/eludium.md" >}}) 100

### **Core Effects and Functionality**
The upgrade provides two primary benefits to your automated workforce:

1.  **Improved Engineer Know-how:** It provides a flat **+2 know-how bonus** to all five classes of Engineers. While other upgrades like AI Engineers or Space Engineers provide higher specific class bonuses, Chronoengineers ensure a baseline efficiency boost across the entire job spectrum.
2.  **Work During Time Skips:** This is the upgrade's most unique feature. It enables Engineers to continue their automated crafting tasks even when you are **combusting Time Crystals** to skip years. 

### **Synergy with Resource Retrieval**
When a time skip occurs, the amount of crafting performed by Chronoengineers is dictated by your **Resource Retrieval** level. 
*   **Automated Conversion:** Just as Resource Retrieval allows you to collect a percentage of your annual production during a skip, Chronoengineers allow your Engineers to "catch up" on their work for that skipped period.
*   **Material Consumption:** It is important to note that this automated crafting **consumes the required materials** just like standard crafting; it does not produce resources "for free" during the skip.

This upgrade is essential for maintaining a stable industrial output during the late-game "shatter engine" phase, ensuring that your refined resource stocks (like Steel, Plate, or Alloy) continue to grow alongside your raw materials as you skip through the centuries.
