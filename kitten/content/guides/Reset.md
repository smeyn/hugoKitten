Resetting is the primary mechanic used to exchange current progress for permanent prestige currencies—**Karma** and [Paragon]( {{< relref "resource/paragon.md" >}})—which provide global multipliers to production and resource storage capacity.

### **Timing Your Reset**
Deciding when to reset depends on your current game phase and prestige goals:
*   **The First Reset**: It is strategically recommended to reset only after researching [Concrete Huts]( {{< relref "upgrade/Concrete_Huts.md" >}}) and reaching at least **130 kittens**. This ensures you gain roughly 60 Paragon, enough to purchase essential early [Metaphysics]( {{< relref "techs/Metaphysics.md" >}}) upgrades like [Diplomacy]( {{< relref "Policy/Diplomacy.md" >}}) or [Golden Ratio]( {{< relref "metaphysics/Golden_Ratio.md" >}}).
*   **Subsequent Early Runs**: Aim for **170+ kittens** on your second run to secure 100 Paragon for the **Divine Proportion** upgrade. 
*   **Prestige Minimums**: You must have more than **35 kittens** to gain Karma and more than **70 kittens** to receive Paragon (one point per kitten over 70). 
*   **The "Slow-Down" Rule**: Generally, you should reset when progress becomes "too slow" or when building costs exceed your current production and storage limits.

### **The Pre-Reset Checklist (BMF)**
Experienced players use the **BMF** mnemonic (Backup, Metas, Faith) to ensure no progress is wasted before a reset:
*   **Backup**: Manually save and export your game data to prevent accidental loss.
*   **Metas (Metaphysics)**: Use your accumulated Paragon to buy permanent upgrades in the Metaphysics tab, particularly **price ratio reductions** like Enlightenment or Renaissance, which make future buildings cheaper.
*   **Faith (The TAP Sequence)**: To maximize spiritual carry-over, perform the **TAP sequence**: **Transcend** (to increase your tier), then **Adore the Galaxy** (to convert worship into [Epiphany]( {{< relref "religion/epiphany.md" >}})), and finally [Praise the Sun]( {{< relref "religion/praise_the_sun.md" >}}).
*   **Population Push**: Max out all housing, including [Huts]( {{< relref "buildings/Hut.md" >}}), [Log Houses]( {{< relref "buildings/Log_House.md" >}}), and [Mansions]( {{< relref "buildings/Mansion.md" >}}), to squeeze out every possible Paragon point.
*   **Resource Conversion**: Sacrifice all unicorns for tears and alicorns for [Time Crystals]( {{< relref "resource/Time_Crystals.md" >}}) before they are lost.

### **Carry-over Mechanics**
Understanding what survives the reset is vital for planning your next run:
*   **Permanent Carry-over**: Karma, Paragon, Metaphysics upgrades, [Epiphany]( {{< relref "religion/epiphany.md" >}}), **Transcendence tiers**, Black Liquid Sorrow (BLS), and **Cryptotheology buildings** are preserved.
*   **Resources Lost**: You lose all standard buildings, technology, and luxury resources like furs, ivory, and spice.
*   **Conditional Carry-over**:
    *   [Chronospheres]( {{< relref "buildings/Chronosphere.md" >}}): Each sphere preserves 1.5% of your non-crafted resources.
    *   [Flux Condensator]( {{< relref "upgrade/Flux_Condensator.md" >}}): This workshop upgrade is required to carry over **crafted resources** like steel, plates, and blueprints.
    *   **Cryochambers**: These allow you to carry over one kitten per chamber, retaining their job skills and experience.

### **Advanced Reset Strategies**
*   **Iron Will (IW) Mode**: In this kittenless mode, the goal is to reset immediately after researching [Archery]( {{< relref "techs/Archery.md" >}}) to accumulate [Zebras]( {{< relref "Civilisations/zebras.md" >}}), which provide passive Catpower for future runs.
*   **Positive Resets**: In the endgame, the goal is to build at least **73 Chronospheres**. At this level, the resources carried over exceed the cost of the spheres themselves, allowing for infinite, self-sustaining growth loops.
