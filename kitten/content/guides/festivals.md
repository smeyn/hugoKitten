In *Kittens Game*, **Festivals** are temporary global events that provide significant growth and production bonuses. They are unlocked via the [Drama and Poetry]( {{< relref "techs/Drama_and_Poetry.md" >}}) technology, which requires 90,000 Science and 5,000 Parchment.

### **Base Effects and Duration**
A standard festival lasts for **one game year** (400 days, which is approximately 13 minutes and 20 seconds of real-time).
*   **Kitten Growth:** The primary benefit of a festival is doubling the rate at which new kittens arrive (from roughly 0.16 to **0.32 kittens per second**). This is essential for quickly filling housing to reach population milestones for [Paragon]( {{< relref "resource/paragon.md" >}}).
*   [Happiness:]( {{< relref "guides/Happiness.md" >}}) Festivals provide a base happiness boost, which improves overall village productivity.

### **Advanced Mechanics (Numeromancy)**
Once you research the [Numeromancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/numeromancy.md" >}}) Metaphysics upgrade, festivals grant powerful **Cycle-specific bonuses**. These are often used to resolve specific late-game resource bottlenecks:
*   [Redmoon:]( {{< relref "planet/Redmoon.md" >}}) **Doubles Unobtainium income** (essential for the space race).
*   [Cath:]( {{< relref "planet/Cath.md" >}}) **Doubles Catpower income** (ideal for mass trading or hunting).
*   [Piscine:]( {{< relref "planet/Piscine.md" >}}) **Doubles Science income**.
*   [Helios:]( {{< relref "planet/Helios.md" >}}) **Doubles Faith income** and increases Unicorn income by 25%.
*   [Kairo:]( {{< relref "planet/Kairo.md" >}}) **Increases Starchart income by 5x**.

### **Building and Policy Synergies**
*   [Brewery:]( {{< relref "buildings/Brewery.md" >}}) This building is specifically designed to enhance festivals. Each Brewery provides **+1% to Festival Happiness** and the **Cycle Bonus**, while slightly increasing kitten arrival speed (+0.1%).
*   **Carnivals (Metaphysics):** This upgrade allows you to **stack festivals**, adding to their duration so you do not have to manually restart them every year.
*   **Carnivale (Policy):** Increases the kitten growth rate during festivals by an additional **30%**, though it also increases luxury resource consumption.
*   **Extravagance (Policy):** Makes rare resources provide significantly more happiness during festivals but consumes them twice as fast.

### **Strategic Utility**
*   **Early Game:** Most guides recommend **skipping Drama and Poetry** (and thus festivals) on your first few runs because the 5,000 Parchment cost is too high for the marginal early-game benefit.
*   **Late Game Paragon Farming:** Festivals are mandatory for "short runs" (approx. 30 minutes) where the goal is to max out population as fast as possible to reset for Paragon.
*   **Resource Spikes:** In the mid-to-late game, players often "shatter" Time Crystals to reach the [Redmoon Cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Redmoon_cycle.md" >}}) and then hold a festival to maximize Unobtainium production.
