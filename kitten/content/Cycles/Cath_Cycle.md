The [Cath cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Cath_Cycle.md" >}}) is the fifth of ten cycles in the *Kittens Game* temporal system, following the Helios cycle and preceding the Redmoon cycle. Each cycle lasts for **five game years** (20,000 ticks), which translates to approximately **1 hour, 6 minutes, and 40 seconds** of real-time.

### **Indicators and Identification**
*   **Glyph:** The cycle is identified by the **⌾** symbol appearing to the left of the year in the calendar.
*   **Color Coding:** The color of the glyph changes to denote the specific year within the five-year Cath cycle: **Red** (Year 0), **Orange-Yellow** (Year 1), **Green** (Year 2), **Blue** (Year 3), and **Violet** (Year 4).

### **Resource Multipliers (Requires Numerology)**
If you have researched the **Numerology** metaphysics upgrade, the Cath cycle provides specific bonuses and penalties to space-based production:
*   [Space Elevators:]( {{< relref "space/space_elevator.md" >}}) Provides a **+100% bonus** to the transferred production bonus, specifically for **Oil**.
*   [Satellites:]( {{< relref "techs/Satellites.md" >}}) Provides a **+100% bonus** to starchart production and a **+100% bonus** to the Satellite observatory science bonus.
*   **Space Stations:** Provides a **+50% bonus** to science production.
*   **Space Beacons:** Imposes a **-50% penalty** to starchart production.

### **Festival Effects (Requires Numeromancy)**
When the [Numeromancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/numeromancy.md" >}}) metaphysics upgrade is researched, holding a **Festival** during the Cath cycle grants a unique global modifier:
*   **Catpower Boost:** All **Catpower income is doubled (x2)** for the duration of the festival. This effect can be further enhanced by building **Breweries**.

### **Strategic Utility**
In the late game, players often use the [Chronoforge]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoforge.md" >}}) to "shatter" Time Crystals, 
skipping through less desirable cycles to reach those that benefit their current bottleneck.
 While [Redmoon]( {{< relref "planet/Redmoon.md" >}}) is often the primary target for Unobtainium farming, the [Cath cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Cath_Cycle.md" >}}) is strategically 
valuable for players needing to maximize **Oil transfer** via Space Elevators or those looking to quickly 
cap [Science]( {{< relref "resource/science.md" >}}) through boosted Satellites and Space Stations. Additionally, the doubled Catpower 
income from festivals makes Cath an ideal time for mass [trading]( {{< relref "guides/trading.md" >}}) or **hunting**.
