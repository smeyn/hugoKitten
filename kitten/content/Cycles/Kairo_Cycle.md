The [Kairo cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Kairo_Cycle.md" >}}) is the tenth and final five-year cycle in the *Kittens Game* time system. Represented by the glyph **℣**, it repeats every 50 years (Years 45–49, 95–99, etc.).

### **Cycle Mechanics and Modifiers**
The effects of the Kairo cycle are only active if you have researched specific Metaphysics technologies:

*   **Numerology Effects:** This technology activates several production and science modifiers for space-based structures, primarily acting as a "fallow" period for general production but a massive boom for starcharts:
    *   [Space Elevators:]( {{< relref "space/space_elevator.md" >}}) Their transferred production bonus is reduced by **-50%**.
    *   [Satellites:]( {{< relref "techs/Satellites.md" >}}) Starchart production and the science bonus provided to Observatories are both reduced by **-25%**.
    *   **Space Stations:** Their science bonus is reduced by **-25%**.
    *   **Space Beacons:** In contrast to the penalties above, their starchart production is boosted by **+400%**.
*   **Numeromancy (Festival) Effects:** If a festival is held during this cycle and Numeromancy is researched, your village receives a massive **x5 multiplier to starchart income**.

### **Relationship to Space Exploration**
The cycle is tied to the [Kairo Mission]( {{< relref "space/Kairo_Mission.md" >}}), which is required to reach the dwarf planet Kairo at the edge of the solar system.
*   **Mission Requirements:** Reaching Kairo requires **5,000 starcharts, 20,000 titanium, 300,000 science, and 7,500 kerosene**.
*   **Space Beacon:** Once Kairo is reached, you can construct Space Beacons. These structures are the primary beneficiary of the cycle's modifiers, providing significant starchart generation and expanded science limits.

### **Strategic Importance**
The Kairo cycle is the **ultimate window for farming starcharts** in the late game. While the penalties to Space Elevators and Satellites can temporarily slow down general resource production and unobtainium gathering, the combination of the **+400% Space Beacon boost** and the **x5 festival bonus** allows players to accumulate the massive starchart reserves needed for high-tier missions like Centaurus or for building large fleets of Trade Ships. 

Advanced players often use a [Chronoforge]( {{< relref "upgrade/Chronoforge.md" >}}) to skip through less productive cycles to land in Kairo when they need to break a starchart bottleneck.
