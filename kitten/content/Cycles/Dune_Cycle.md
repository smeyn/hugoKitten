The [Dune cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Dune_Cycle.md" >}}) is the seventh in the sequence of ten five-year cycles within the *Kittens Game* time system. Represented by the glyph **⍫**, it repeats every 50 years (starting at Year 30 of a millennium, then Year 80, 130, etc.). Its primary function is to significantly boost industrial outputs related to the planet for which it is named.

### **Cycle Mechanics and Effects**
Like other cycles, Dune’s effects are activated only after researching specific late-game Metaphysics technologies:

*   **Numerology Effects:** This technology activates modifiers for space-based structures located on the planet Dune and the Umbra black hole:
    *   [Planet Crackers:]( {{< relref "space/planet_cracker.md" >}}) Uranium output is increased by **+10%**.
    *   **Hydraulic Fracturers:** Oil output is increased by **+50%**.
    *   **HR Harvesters:** Energy production is reduced by **-25%**.
*   **Numeromancy (Festival) Effects:** If a festival is held while the Dune cycle is active and Numeromancy is researched, your village receives a **2x multiplier (doubled income)** for [Uranium]( {{< relref "resource/uranium.md" >}}).

### **Relationship to Planet Dune**
The cycle is tied to the [Dune Mission]( {{< relref "space/Dune_Mission.md" >}}), which is unlocked after the Moon Mission. Reaching Dune requires **1,000 starcharts, 7,000 titanium, 175,000 science, and 75 kerosene**. Once reached, you can construct the structures affected by the cycle’s modifiers:
*   [Planet Cracker:]( {{< relref "space/planet_cracker.md" >}}) The primary source of automated [Uranium]( {{< relref "resource/uranium.md" >}}) production.
*   **Hydraulic Fracturer:** Provides a high-pressure stream of **Oil**.
*   [Spice Refinery:]( {{< relref "space/spice_refinery.md" >}}) Refines sand into [Spice]( {{< relref "resource/spice.md" >}}), a rare resource that improves happiness.

### **Strategic Importance**
The Dune cycle is a critical window for resource management in the late mid-game and early space eras:
*   **Uranium Farming:** Uranium is essential for powering [Reactors]( {{< relref "buildings/Reactor.md" >}}), funding [Nuclear Smelters]( {{< relref "upgrade/Nuclear_Smelters.md" >}}) (for titanium), and fueling [Lunar Outposts]( {{< relref "planet/lunar_outpost.md" >}}) (to produce unobtainium). The 2x festival bonus makes this cycle the most efficient time to stockpile uranium.
*   **Oil Stabilization:** Because Hydraulic Fracturers receive a **50% production boost** during this cycle, it is an ideal time to fill your oil caps to build expensive space structures like **Research Vessels** or **Moon Bases**.
*   **Energy Management:** Players heavily reliant on **HR Harvesters** for power (common in the deep late game) must be wary of the **-25% energy penalty** during this cycle, which could lead to an energy deficit and global production penalties.
*   **Progression Flow:** In the sequence of cycles, Dune acts as a mirrored opposite to the [Umbra cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Umbra_Cycle.md" >}}), which penalizes Dune structure output while boosting Umbra structures. **Shattering Time Crystals** is often used to skip Umbra and land in cycles like Dune or Redmoon to maximize resource yield.
