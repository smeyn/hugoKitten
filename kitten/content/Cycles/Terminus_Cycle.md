The [Terminus cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/Terminus_Cycle.md" >}}) (often referred to in tables as the **T-minus cycle**) is the ninth in the sequence of ten five-year cycles within the *Kittens Game* time system. Represented by the glyph **⍝**, it repeats every 50 years (beginning at Year 40 of a millennium, then Year 90, 140, etc.).

### **Cycle Mechanics and Effects**
The effects of the Terminus cycle are activated only after researching specific Metaphysics technologies:

*   **Numerology Effects:** Researching **Numerology** activates production and storage modifiers for space-based structures:
    *   [Sunlifter:]( {{< relref "space/sunlifter.md" >}}) Energy output is reduced by **-50%**.
    *   **Cryostations:** Storage capacity is increased by **+20%**.
*   **Numeromancy (Festival) Effects:** If a festival is held during this cycle and [Numeromancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/numeromancy.md" >}}) is researched, the village receive a **2x multiplier (doubled income)** for **Oil**.

### **Space Exploration Context**
The cycle is named after the planet **Terminus**, which is reached through the [T-minus Mission]( {{< relref "space/T-minus_Mission.md" >}}).
*   **Planet Description:** Terminus is described as a supermassive ice giant located at the far end of the Helios solar system.
*   **Mission Requirements:** Reaching it requires **2,500 starcharts, 12,000 titanium, 225,000 science, and 750 kerosene**.
*   **Unlocks:** Completing the mission unlocks the [Kairo Mission]( {{< relref "space/Kairo_Mission.md" >}}), the **Cryostation** structure, and access to the **T-Minus planet**.

### **Strategic Importance**
In the late game, the Terminus cycle is highly valued for its bonus to **Unobtainium storage capacity**:
*   **UO Cap Scaling:** Because Cryostations are one of only two structures that provide Unobtainium storage, the **+20% storage bonus** from this cycle is essential for players attempting to reach the **8.14 billion Unobtainium cap** needed to build 100 Chronospheres.
*   **Oil Management:** The 2x festival bonus makes this cycle an ideal time to stockpile oil, especially when preparing to build expensive space structures like **Research Vessels**.
*   **Energy Warning:** Players must be cautious of the **-50% penalty to Sunlifter energy output** during this cycle, which can lead to a sudden energy deficit and global production penalties if their grid is not sufficiently diversified.
