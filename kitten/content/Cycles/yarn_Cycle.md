The [Yarn cycle]( {{< relref "Cycles/yarn_Cycle.md" >}}) is one of the ten five-year cycles in the *Kittens Game* time system. Each cycle is associated with a specific glyph and provides various production modifiers and festival bonuses once certain late-game technologies are researched.

### **Cycle Mechanics and Effects**
The effects of the Yarn cycle become active only after researching specific Metaphysics technologies:

*   **Numerology Effect:** When **Numerology** is researched, the Yarn cycle provides a **+100% boost to catnip production from Hydroponics**. Conversely, it imposes a **-50% penalty to starchart production from Research Vessels**.
*   **Numeromancy Effect:** If [Numeromancy]( {{< relref "metaphysics/numeromancy.md" >}}) is researched, holding a **festival** during the Yarn cycle results in **doubled culture income** (x2).
*   **Timing:** As the third cycle in the sequence, it begins at Year 10 of a millennium and repeats every 50 years (Years 10–14, 60–64, etc.).

### **Space Exploration Context**
The cycle is named after the **planet Yarn**, a Class M planet with a moderate climate and oxygen atmosphere.
*   **Mission:** The Yarn Mission is unlocked after the Helios mission and requires **7,500 starcharts, 35,000 titanium, 350,000 science, and 12,000 kerosene**.
*   **Planet Structures:** Once reached, you can build **Terraforming Stations** (which increase your maximum kitten population by +1) and [Hydroponics]( {{< relref "techs/Hydroponics.md" >}}) systems. 
*   [Hydroponics:]( {{< relref "techs/Hydroponics.md" >}}) These structures provide a **+10% bonus to maximum catnip** and a **+2.5% boost to catnip production**. During the Yarn cycle, this production bonus is significantly enhanced by the Numerology modifier.

### **Strategic Importance**
In the extreme endgame, **terraforming Yarn** is a key strategy for maximizing kitten population. By building a massive number of Terraforming Stations, players can reach population levels high enough to generate "unreasonable" amounts of [Paragon]( {{< relref "resource/paragon.md" >}}) (up to 120,000 per RL-day) during long runs. Additionally, the cycle’s festival bonus makes it an ideal time to grind [culture]( {{< relref "resource/culture.md" >}}) if you are still using it for early-to-mid-game crafts like Manuscripts or Compendiums.
